
Republican National Committee Chairman Lee Atwater will speak Thursday night at a fund-raiser for the Richard Nixon Library and Birthplace Foundation. 
Those who attend the event will be able to see the $25-million Nixon library at the halfway point of construction. The library is scheduled to open in June, 1990. 
Supervisor Gaddi H. Vasquez will be host for the event, which will be held from 5 to 7 p.m. For information, call (714) 834-1484.
