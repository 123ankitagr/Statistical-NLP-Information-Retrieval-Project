
Seton Healthcare Group has acquired the Medised brand from Macarthy
Laboratories for Pounds 3.5m cash.
Medised is a leading over-the-counter product for colds and flu in children.
The acquisition represents another step in Seton's strategy of building a
portfolio of established healthcare brands, which the group can promote
through its sales network to pharmacists and consumers.
In the 12 months to June 30, unaudited sales attributable to the Medised
brand were about Pounds 725,000.
