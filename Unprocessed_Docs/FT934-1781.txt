
Average earnings rose an underlying 3 per cent in the year to October,
unchanged from September and still the slowest rate of growth for 26 years.
Earnings growth in manufacturing held steady at 4.25 per cent, while the
government-imposed 1.5 per cent limit on public-sector pay rises helped keep
service-sector earnings growth down to 2.25 per cent.
