
NatWest Investment Management has appointed Colin Harte, 37, as a director
in the active fixed income area, with responsibility for managing fixed
income portfolios for pension funds.
He joins from Worldinvest, where he was director and head of fixed income,
responsible for managing Dollars 3.5bn of assets, primarily for US pension
funds. He has also worked for Citibank, Hill Samuel, Morgan Stanley and
Guardian Royal Exchange.
