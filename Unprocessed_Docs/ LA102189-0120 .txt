
Grant 20, Van Nuys 6 -- Shawn Booker rushed for 134 yards in 24 carries and scored two touchdowns to lead Grant at Van Nuys. 
Vatsana Phounsavath gave Van Nuys (2-3, 2-1) a 6-0 lead in the first quarter with an 11-yard touchdown run, but Grant took a 7-6 lead in the second quarter on Booker's one-yard touchdown run. Booker scored on a five-yard run in the third quarter and Grant (6-0, 3-0) closed out the scoring in the final quarter when Marlowe Lewis hit Josh Kesselman with a 10-yard touchdown pass.
