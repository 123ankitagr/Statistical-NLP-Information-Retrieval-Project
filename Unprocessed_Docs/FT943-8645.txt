
DENCORA has bought a 112-year lease on Spectrum House, Basingstoke, for
Pounds 2.64m. The consideration will be satisfied by the issue of 785,000
shares to the vendor, Hill Samuel Property Unit Trust, which have been
placed with institutions, and by existing bank facilities. Current rent
receivable is Pounds 305,000 a year and the annual ground rent is Pounds
61,000.
