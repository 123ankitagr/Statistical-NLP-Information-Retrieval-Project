
Delta Cuts L.A.-to-San Francisco Fares: Delta Air Lines, following the lead of California's other major carriers, slashed fares between San Francisco and Los Angeles to as low as $58 round trip. 
The $58 fare applies only to tickets purchased 21 days in advance for travel on any day except Friday and Sunday. Seven-day advance tickets can be purchased for $78 on off-peak days and $84 on Fridays and Sundays. One-day advance fares of $98 round trip are available seven days a week.
