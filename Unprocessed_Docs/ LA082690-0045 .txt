
"Moving Buildings for Profit" is the title of a new self-teaching investment manual written by Jim Dunham, president of the American Architectural Preservation Group Inc. 
In the 200-page manual Dunham explains how to find lots, how to evaluate the moving contractor, get free buildings, arrange financing and walk through the processing procedure. Included are checklists, forms and examples of documents used in the relocation process. 
The manual is available by writing The House Relocator, 2600 S. Hoover St., Los Angeles, Calif. 90007. Cost is $85, tax and shipping included.
