
A prep all-star football game will be played tonight at 7:30 at Mira Mesa High in San Diego. 
The teams, representing Los Angeles City and San Diego County school, are composed of 44 recently graduated seniors. 
Bill Redell, formerly of Crespi High in Encino, will coach the Los Angeles squad, which is 0-3 in the series. 
Quarterback Armin Youngblood of Carson will be reunited with his two top receivers, Michael Ross and Titus Winans. The running backs will be Mark Harper from Santa Monica and Michael and Gregory Jones from Van Nuys Montclair Prep.
