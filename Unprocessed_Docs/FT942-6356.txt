
There is further evidence of the demise of the gentlemen's agreement between
New York and London law firms to keep off each other's home turf: Milbank
Tweed of the US has lured away two Clifford Chance lawyers, Kenneth
MacRitchie and Nick Buckworth, to work on global project finance in its
London office. Their arrival will boost Milbank's ability to do global
project financings governed by English law.
