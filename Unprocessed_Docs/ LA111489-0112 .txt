
Kramer Kin Is Miss Golden Globe: The Hollywood Foreign Press Assn. has named its 1990 Miss Golden Globe. She is Katharine Kramer, the 20-year-old singer-actress daughter of director Stanley Kramer. She will appear at various group events, including the 47th annual Golden Globe Awards Jan. 20. Kramer has appeared in a one-woman show, "The Colors of Myself," and has a two-week return date at the Hollywood Roosevelt Cinegrill coming up in January following an earlier sellout engagement there.
