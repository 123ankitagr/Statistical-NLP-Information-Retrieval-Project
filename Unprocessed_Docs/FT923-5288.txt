
MR ANDREW THREADGOLD, chief executive and director of securities investment
at PosTel, the UK's largest pension fund, is to head the Australian and
overseas investment business of AMP, Australia's largest insurance company.
AMP has Pounds 25bn under management, exceeding PosTel's funds by about
Pounds 5bn. PosTel includes the pension schemes of the Post Office and
British Telecommunications.
