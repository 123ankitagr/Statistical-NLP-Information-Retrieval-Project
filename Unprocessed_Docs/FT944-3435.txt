
Guinness Peat, the UK investment vehicle of New Zealand entrepreneur Sir Ron
Brierley, has acquired 7.5m ordinary shares, representing 26.8 per cent of
the issued share capital, of Turners & Growers.
The NZDollars 8.4m (Pounds 3.4m) deal gives Guinness Peat a total of 28.8
per cent in the New Zealand company, which deals in the marketing of fruit
and produce and vehicle auctioneering.
Turners & Growers reported a net loss of NZDollars 1.57m for the year to
March 31, prior to an abnormal loss of NZDollars 3.88m. Assets stood at
NZDollars 49.2m.
