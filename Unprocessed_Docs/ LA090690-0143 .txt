
Here is a list of the national holidays for the 1990-91 school year. All North County schools will be closed on these days unless otherwise noted in the calendar information for each district. 
Nov. 12: Veteran's Day 
Nov. 22-23: Thanksgiving 
Dec. 24-Jan. 4: Winter recess 
Jan. 21: Martin Luther King Jr. Day 
Feb. 11: Lincoln's Birthday 
Feb. 18: Washington's Birthday 
March 25-29: Spring Break 
May 27: Memorial Day
