
Sir Derek Hornby has been appointed non-executive director at Sedgwick
Group, the insurance broker, with effect from yesterday. He is 62 and is
chairman of the British Overseas Trade Board; he headed Rank-Xerox from 1984
to 1990.
Mr Hugh Collum, finance director of SmithKline Beecham, a non-executive
director since 1987, and Mr Rupert Hambro, managing director of JO Hambro
and chairman of JO Hambro Magan, are both retiring.
