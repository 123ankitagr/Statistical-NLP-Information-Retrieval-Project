
Some hacks go to extreme lengths to obtain the confidential information
dispensed at creditors' meetings. At a recent Olympia & York meeting, for
example, one Canadian journalist tried to pass himself off as a waiter.
However, Reuters yesterday had the distinction of having a completely
legitimate reason for sitting through one of nine sessions of Canary Wharf's
creditors. The news agency is owed money by Canary Wharf - which only goes
to show that every cloud has a silver lining.
