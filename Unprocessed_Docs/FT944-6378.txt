
Toray Industries, the Japanese textile maker, plans to set up a textile
printing joint venture with Komatsu Seiren, a Japanese printing company of
synthetic fabrics. The venture, with total capital of Y4.5bn (Pounds 28.9m),
will invest Y1.1bn in a polyester filament printing plant, to start
operations in Nottingham by 1996. The plant will employ 50 local workers and
Japanese staff, Toray said.
