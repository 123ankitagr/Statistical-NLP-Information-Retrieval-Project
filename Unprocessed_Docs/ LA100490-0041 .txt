
Authors marking AIDS Awareness Month will hold a round table to discuss their books and acquired immune deficiency syndrome at 7:30 tonight in the West Hollywood Library, 715 N. San Vicente Blvd. 
Reporter Steven Gendel of Channel 4 news will act as moderator for the three featured authors who include Tolbert McCarroll author of "Morning Glory Babies," Michael Nava, author of "How Town," and Holly Uyemoto, who wrote "Rebel Without a Clue." All three books contain AIDS themes. 
In addition to book discussion, the round table authors will be available for book signings and to answer questions. 
For more information, call the West Hollywood Library, (213) 652-5340.
