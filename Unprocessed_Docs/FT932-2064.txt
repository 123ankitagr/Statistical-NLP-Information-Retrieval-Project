
LAING SCOTLAND has won a Pounds 5.3m contract for the construction of the
Dunfermline Building Society headquarters at Carnegie Campus, part of the
Pitreavie business park.
The work involves a two-storey headquarters building, a single-storey
ancillary building, access road, car park and landscaping. High quality
materials will be used for the main building, which comprises offices,
computer suites and training areas.
