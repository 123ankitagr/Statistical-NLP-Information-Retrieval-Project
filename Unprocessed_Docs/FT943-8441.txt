
Net asset value at Angerstein Underwriting Trust, set up last year to
provide corporate capital to the Lloyd's insurance market on a limited
liability basis, was 90.28p per share at May 31.
For its first accounting period - from September 17 1993 to May 31 - net
revenue after tax was Pounds 819,000.
Earnings per share came out at 1.21p and a dividend of 1.2p is declared. The
board said it intends total dividends for the 12-month period to be not less
than 2p.
