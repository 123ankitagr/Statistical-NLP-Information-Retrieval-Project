
DY Davies, the USM-quoted architect, moved nearer to the black in the year
to April 30. Pre-tax losses of Pounds 56,000 compared with a deficit last
time of Pounds 793,000.
Turnover of Pounds 5.55m (Pounds 7.63m) included Pounds 5.05m (Pounds 4.52m)
from architecture and allied services. Operating profit rose to Pounds
278,000 (Pounds 128,000).
Losses per share were cut to 0.9p (11.5p).
