
Construction has started on a $25-million town center in Bonsall known as River Village. 
CentrumInvest, a Swedish company known for its shopping/entertainment/hospitality centers throughout Northern Europe, is developing River Village on 24 acres at Mission Road and California 76 overlooking the San luis Rey River. It is the firm's first project in the United states 
The center is being designed as a gathering place for residents of Fallbrook, Bonsall and surrounding communities. It will have 155,000 square feet of leasable space. Phase 1 is expected to be completed by next summer. 
Architects/Larson Carpenter is project architect, and Nowell-Thompson ASLA & Associates is the landscape architect. Ninetman Construction Co. is general contractor, and the Neville Co. is project manager.
