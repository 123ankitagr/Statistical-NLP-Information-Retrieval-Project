
CAR production last month was nearly 18 per cent higher than in July last
year, according to the Society of Motor Manufacturers and Traders. Last
month 120,740 cars were produced bringing the total for the year so far to
807,487 - a rise of 1.39 per cent on the same period last year.
Commercial vehicle production in July was was 24,073, up 47.55 per cent on
the same period last year.
Sales of new K-registration cars in the first 10 days of this month were
223,283, a fall of 1.8 per cent compared with the same period last year.
