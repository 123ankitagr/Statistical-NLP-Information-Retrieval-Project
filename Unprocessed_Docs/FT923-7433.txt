
Demand continued strong and general reports the Tea Broker's Association.
Landed brightest East African remained fully firm but good mediums showed
some irregularity following quality. Other descriptions tended dearer
particularly Tanzanias and the brighter teas from Central Africa. Offshore
good demand at dearer rates with brighter Kenyas often showing advances of 4
to 6p per kilo. The highest price realised this week was 203p for a Rwanda
pd. Quotations: quality 175p, nom good medium 140p, medium 127p, low medium
80p.
