
Cal State Fullerton, which has won four of its last five basketball games, will play University of the Pacific in a Big West Conference game at 7:35 tonight at Titan Gym. 
The Titans (12-11 overall, 7-7 in the conference) were pushed to overtime in their first game against Pacific this season, but won, 79-75. 
Pacific (5-18, 1-13), since beating New Mexico State Jan. 14, has 11 consecutive losses, including four in overtime. ROBYN NORWOOD
