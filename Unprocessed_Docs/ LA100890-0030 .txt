
Buddies as Talk Show Hosts: "Kelly and Gail," a new half-hour morning show that will focus on love, sex and relationships, debuts Oct. 22 at 9 a.m. on Channel 4. Hosted by KNBC anchorwoman Kelly Lange and TV comedy writer Gail Parent -- longtime friends who have seen each other through all sorts of boyfriends, marriages and family crises -- the show will air live weekdays before a studio audience.CLAUDIA PUIG
