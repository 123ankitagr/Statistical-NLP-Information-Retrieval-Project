Husband-Husband : French film director Roger Vadim , former husband to film actresses Brigitte Bardot , Catherine Deneuve , Jane Fonda and Annette Stroyberg , married another actress , Marie-Christine Barrault , Friday . Vadim , 62 , is best known for launching Bardot 's career in the late 1950s and helping launch that of Fonda in the 1960s . Barrault , 46 , is a classical theater actress best known internationally for her lead role in the film comedy `` Cousin-Cousine . '' BETH KLEID
