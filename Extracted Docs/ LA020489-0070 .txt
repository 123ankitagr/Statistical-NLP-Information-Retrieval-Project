max laemmle the man who loved movies was a major force in los angeles film culture not only for the films he presented but for his warm and generous collaboration with other film organizations it was with the help of laemmle who died jan 20 that the american film institute was able to reorganize the los angeles international film festival in 1987 replacing filmex and present it at his los feliz cinema his collaboration with the afi continued in specialized festivals at his santa monica cinema devoted to european british u s independent and documentary films he will be greatly missed ken wlaschin director afi la filmfest gary mcvey associate director
