Retired Regulator to Run Mercury S & L : William J. Crawford , who retired in April as state S & L commissioner , will be placed in charge of insolvent Mercury Savings & Loan Assn . in Huntington Beach today , a spokesman for federal regulators said . Kevin Shields , a spokesman for the Resolution Trust Corp. 's regional office in Denver , said that Crawford will be named managing agent of Mercury , which was seized by federal regulators in February .
