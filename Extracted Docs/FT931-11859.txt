KIER INTERNATIONAL 's Caribbean construction company has been awarded two contracts with a combined total exceeding Pounds 12m . The first , valued at Pounds 11.5m is to construct a corporate headquarters in Kingston , Jamaica for Mutual Life Assurance . Built on reinforced concrete foundations , the seven-storey building will comprise a structural frame of in situ reinforced concrete columns and precast beams . The second contract , valued at Pounds 800,000 , comprises piling , earthworks and construction of foundations for a 35MW gas turbine plant together with administration buildings and storage tanks at Hunts Bay in Jamaica .
