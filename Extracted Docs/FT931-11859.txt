kier international's caribbean construction company has been awarded two contracts with a combined total exceeding pounds 12 m the first valued at pounds 11.5 m is to construct a corporate headquarters in kingston jamaica for mutual life assurance built on reinforced concrete foundations the seven-storey building will comprise a structural frame of in situ reinforced concrete columns and precast beams the second contract valued at pounds 800,000, comprises piling earthworks and construction of foundations for a 35 mw gas turbine plant together with administration buildings and storage tanks at hunts bay in jamaica
