Phyllis McGrady , executive producer of the new ABC News prime-time series that Sam Donaldson and Diane Sawyer will co-anchor , is leaving the job because of a `` serious illness '' in her family , ABC has announced . McGrady , appointed to the job in February , was immediately succeeded by Richard Kaplan , executive producer of Ted Koppel 's `` Nightline '' for the last five years . Claudia Puig
