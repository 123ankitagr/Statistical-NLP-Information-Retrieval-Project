possibly not the best time to be associated with the blood giving business but hartlepool brewer camerons had the best of intentions when it launched its latest marketing ploy a pint of ruby red strongarm bitter for every new blood donor on teesside managing director derek andrews idea was warmly welcomed by the blood transfusion service whose only condition was that the beer be drunk off the premises sadly andrews invitation to tea and biscuit manufacturers to demonstrate similar spunk leaves a nasty taste in the mouth just at the moment
