Fred Hoover , an assistant baseball coach at Cal State Fullerton , has been named assistant baseball coach at UC Irvine . Hoover , 59 , will work primarily with pitchers at Irvine . Hoover had been an assistant coach at Fullerton for the past two seasons . Before that , he was head baseball coach at Golden West College for 22 years , compiling a 478-269 record . At Golden West , his teams won seven conference titles and finished second seven times . At Irvine , Hoover replaces Robin Dreizler , who recently accepted a position with UCLA .
