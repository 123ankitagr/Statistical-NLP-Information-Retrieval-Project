A Los Angeles man was killed , his passenger was in serious condition and an unidentified young woman was critically injured when the woman drove the wrong way on Interstate 5 early Sunday and crashed into another car . The unidentified woman was driving north in the southbound lanes of Interstate 5 , near Coronado Avenue , about 1:50 a.m. , according to San Diego police . Her vehicle collided head-on with a car driven by Jose Medina of Los Angeles . Medina , 28 , died at the scene . His passenger , Sandra Alvarado , 21 , and the unidentified woman , thought to be in her 20s , were taken to UC San Diego Medical Center . The woman carried no identification .
