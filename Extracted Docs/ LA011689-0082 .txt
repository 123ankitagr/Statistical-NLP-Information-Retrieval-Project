Janet Evans , who won three gold medals in last summer 's Olympics , won the 400-meter individual medley Sunday at the Southern California Swimming Q-Meet at Belmont Plaza in Long Beach . Evans , of Placentia , finished in 4:12.81 . The meet concludes today with Evans swimming the 1,650-meter freestyle . Preliminary heats start at 9 a.m. with the finals at 5 p.m .
