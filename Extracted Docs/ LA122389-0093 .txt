A Feb. 13 trial was set during an arraignment in Stockton , Calif. , for Mike Blatt , former Seattle Seahawk general manager who is charged with the murder of a former business partner . Blatt , 44 , is accused of hiring the murder of Lawrence Carnegie . Carnegie , a real estate broker , was slain last Feb. 28 by an arrow shot from a crossbow after being lured to an empty home by a caller who said he was considering buying the house .
