London 's Players ' Theatre will present its production , `` British Music Hall , '' at Ambassador Auditorium , 300 W. Green St. , Pasadena , at 8:30 p.m. Saturday and 2 p.m. Sunday . Tickets are $ 19.50 , $ 21.50 and $ 22.50 . For more than 50 years , London 's Players ' Theatre has been presenting turn-of-the-century British music hall , a Victorian-style vaudeville offering comedy , songs , dances and audience sing-alongs . The performances mark the first American tour by the all-English cast under the direction of Eric W. McConnell , Dominic Le Foe and Daniel Rutherford . The event is presented by Ambassador Foundation and sponsored by Ambassador College .
