T-Bill Rates Rise : Yields on seven-year Treasury notes rose in Wednesday 's auction to the highest level in six months . The average yield was 8.08 % , up from 7.89 % at the last auction on July 12 . It was the highest rate since seven-year notes averaged 9.39 % on April 12 . The notes will carry a coupon interest rate of 8 % , with each $ 10,000 in face value selling for $ 9,957.90 . A total of $ 7.5 billion in notes was sold out of bids totaling $ 18.5 billion .
