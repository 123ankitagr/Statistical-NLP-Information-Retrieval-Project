Former Lucky Stores chief executive John M. Lillie has been named to the new position of president and chief operating officer of American President Cos. , an Oakland-based ocean and rail transportation company . Lillie , 53 , was chairman and chief executive of California supermarket giant Lucky Stores Inc. when it was bought by American Stores Co. of Salt Lake City in 1988 . He left the company in 1989 after the $ 2.5-billion merger was completed and joined the venture capital firm Sequoia Associates in Menlo Park , Calif. , as a general partner . At American President , he will oversee both the company 's Pacific Basin shipping line , American President Lines and its domestic freight business , American President Domestic .
