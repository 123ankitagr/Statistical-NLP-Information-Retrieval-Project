There are expectations of a fare war between Asian airlines , following Singapore Airlines ' announcement that it is cutting fares for first- and business-class seats on regional and some intercontinental routes by 10-20 per cent , writes Kieran Cooke in Kuala Lumpur . A return Singapore-Hong Kong business-class trip on SIA will fall to SDollars 1,770 ( Pounds 753 ) from SDollars 2,082 . Other carriers in the region are expected to follow SIA 's example .
