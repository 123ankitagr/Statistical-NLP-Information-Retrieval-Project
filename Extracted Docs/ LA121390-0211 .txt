2 Firms Owned By Harry W. Hay File Chapter 7 : The companies owned by Hay , a Huntington Beach businessman accused by the state Department of Insurance of operating illegal health insurance companies that defrauded small-business owners of some $ 9 million , have filed for a Chapter 7 bankruptcy liquidation . In separate Dec. 5 filings in U.S. Bankruptcy Court in Santa Ana , Health Data Processing Insurance Administrators Inc. listed debts of $ 119,712 and California Development Maintenance Assn . Inc. listed debts of $ 1,477 .
