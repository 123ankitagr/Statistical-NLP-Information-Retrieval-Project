Huttons Kiwi , part of the Brierley group , is buying 18.4 per cent of Asian New Zealand Meat for NZDollars 9.8m , writes Terry Hall in Wellington . Asian NZ , which is 65 per cent owned by the farmers co-operative , the New Zealand Meat Producers Board , is New Zealand 's biggest exporter of meat related products to Japan . Huttons Kiwi , which was partially floated to the public in October , is financing the deal through a share issue .
