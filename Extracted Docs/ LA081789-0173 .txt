parents concerned about drug and alcohol abuse can get two pamphlets on these problems from the local office of state sen john seymour r-anaheim the booklets offer guidelines on what to look for and what to do if a child has a substance abuse problem said seymour who is chairman of the senate select committee on substance abuse the guides are entitled the facts about alcohol abuse how parents can protect their children and the facts about drug abuse how parents can protect their children to get either brochure write to seymour 2150 towne centre place suite 205, anaheim calif 92806
