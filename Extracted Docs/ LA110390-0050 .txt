A 27-year-old tile setter has been indicted on murder and robbery charges by the Ventura County grand jury in connection with the July 3 slaying of a Port Hueneme man . Anthony Eugene Diaz was indicted by the grand jury in the death of Bruce Eugene Pope , 62 , who was found in his ransacked condominium , beaten and strangled with a telephone cord . A television , videocassette recorder , computer , camera and cash were missing from Pope 's home . The indictment charges Diaz with murder and robbery and a special circumstance of murder during a robbery , which would allow prosecutors to seek the death penalty . Police have said they believe Pope and Diaz met each other by chance and that Diaz was invited to Pope 's condominium . MACK REED
