The UCLA women 's volleyball team , ranked second in the country , will try to remain undefeated in the Pacific 10 Conference when it plays host to Stanford tonight at 7 at Pauley Pavilion . Stanford , which opened the season ranked second , has fallen to sixth nationally after losing to unranked Oregon last Saturday . The Cardinal is 8-3 overall . The Bruins , whose only loss was to top-ranked Hawaii on Sept. 8 , are 9-1 overall and lead the Pac-10 at 4-0 . The Bruins have won 22 consecutive Pac-10 matches over the last two seasons , a conference record .
