Two Chicago police officers who allegedly abandoned two black youngsters in a Southwest Side neighborhood where they were then beaten by a gang of whites were indicted on misconduct and battery charges . The two officers , Kathleen A. Moore , 31 , and James J. Serio , 35 , allegedly picked up the two black youths for curfew violations on Aug. 15 in the Bridgeport neighborhood where Mayor Richard M. Daley lives , slapped them around and then dropped them off in Canaryville , where they were beaten .
