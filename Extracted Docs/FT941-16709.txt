Mr Anthony Abrahams , managing director of the UK marketmaking operations of Smith New Court , is to retire from his executive duties in April . He will remain on the board as a non-executive director . Yesterday Mr Abrahams sold 175,000 SNC shares at 415p each , while his wife Celia sold another 25,000 at the same price . The couple still own 106,027 shares . Mr David Marks , currently deputy managing director , succeeds Mr Abrahams , while Mr Colin Taylor and Mr Adrian Pinkus become deputy managing directors .
