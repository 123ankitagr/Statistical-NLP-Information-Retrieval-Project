UPS Awarded Cargo Route to Japan : The U.S. Transportation Department selected United Parcel Service Co. to operate a new all-cargo route to Japan . Emery Worldwide Airlines was awarded backup authority . The new route was authorized in an agreement last year between Japan and the United States calling for a substantial expansion of air service between the two countries . UPS spokesman Ken Sternad said the company , which plans to operate six flights a week , had not yet placed a value on the route .
