A mural by Fairfax-area senior citizens that depicts Melrose Avenue at dusk will be dedicated Sunday at the Fairfax Senior Citizen Center , 7929 Melrose Ave. More than 30 senior citizens worked on the mural , which consists of 60 panels of butcher paper pasted together . A year in the making , the mural hangs from the back wall of the senior citizen center 's stage . The volunteer artists , instructor Saundra Moss , Los Angeles City Councilman Zev Yaroslavsky , local merchants and city Recreation and Parks officials are expected to attend the dedication ceremony , which will begin at 11 a.m .
