WHAT is the maximum weekly amount which I could pay my wife for assistance in my business for the last tax year without having to pay National Insurance stamps . The weekly exemption limit for wages actually paid in 1991-92 was Pounds 51.99 ; the corresponding limit for the current tax year ( 1992-93 ) is Pounds 53.99 .
