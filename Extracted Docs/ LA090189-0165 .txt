French industrialist Bernard Tapie , the president of Olympique Marseille , was quoted as saying today that Argentine soccer star Diego Maradona is ready to break his contract with Napoli and transfer to the French club . `` A group of financiers is ready to give Maradona $ 35 million so that he can settle problems related to his breach of contract with Napoli and play with another club by mid-October , '' Tapie was quoted as saying in Milan 's sports daily Gazzetta dello Sport . The newspaper said Tapie made his remarks on his yacht , which was cruising in the Mediterranean .
