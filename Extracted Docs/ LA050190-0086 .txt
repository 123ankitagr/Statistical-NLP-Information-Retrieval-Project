Union Texas to Explore Sale : Houston-based Union Texas Petroleum Holdings Inc. , one of the nation 's largest independent oil companies , said its board authorized an executive committee of directors to explore a sale of the company and other alternatives to enhance the value of the firm . Last week , Union Texas reported first-quarter net income of $ 31 million , down from $ 50 million a year ago . Revenue rose to $ 311 million from $ 307 million .
