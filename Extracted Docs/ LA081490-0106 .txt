Deloitte Leaves First Executive : The accounting firm Deloitte & Touche has removed itself as auditor of First Executive Corp. , the battered Los Angeles insurance company . Neither First Executive nor the accounting firm gave a reason . First Executive recently hired a number of Deloitte & Touche executives . Once one of former junk-bond financier Michael Milken 's best customers , First Executive lost $ 775.6 million last year , largely because the value of its junk bonds plunged .
