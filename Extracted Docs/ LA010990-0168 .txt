Steve Guy of New Zealand upset second-seeded Czech Miloslav Mecir 6-3 , 6-3 today in the first round of the Benson and Hedges New Zealand Open tennis championships . He will next face compatriot Brett Steven , a 6-3 , 6-0 winner over Finn Veli Paloheimo . American Glenn Layendecker also scored an upset today , ousting No . 4 seed Kelly Evernden of New Zealand , 6-2 , 6-4 . Top-seed Andrei Chesnokov of the Soviet Union , ranked 18th in the world , struggled past West German qualifier Marcus Zoecke 6-7 ( 5-7 ) , 7-6 ( 7-4 ) , 7-5 , and American Scott Davis , the eighth seed , rallied to defeat Swede Christian Bergstrom 1-6 , 6-4 , 6-2 . Americans Jimmy Arias and Richey Reneberg also advanced to the last 16 . Arias downed Italian Claudio Pistolesi 6-3 , 6-3 , and Reneberg breezed past Israeli Gilad Bloom 6-3 , 6-2 . In Sydney , Australia , meanwhile , rain washed out all play today in the New South Wales Open tennis championships .
