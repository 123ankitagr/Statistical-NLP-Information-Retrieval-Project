The East German Investment Trust yesterday reported a net asset value of DM2.71 per share at its December 31 period end . The trust , formed to invest predominantly in unquoted companies located or operating in the former East Germany , came to the main market on February 25 last year via a placing by County NatWest Wood Mackenzie and Berliner Bank . Net revenue for the period to end-December amounted to DM2.53m ( Pounds 880,000 ) for earnings per share of DM0.0655 . A dividend of DM0.05 is declared for payment on May .
