A 25-year-old man was arrested and charged in the murder of an elderly Rowland Heights woman who was found dead in her apartment last month , authorities said Thursday . Tony Anthony Hardin was arrested Tuesday by Los Angeles County sheriff 's homicide detectives and arraigned Thursday in Citrus Municipal Court , Deputy Hal Grant said . Hardin was charged in the murder of Norma Barber , 66 , whose body was found April 8 by her son at her apartment at 1900 Fullerton Road in the unincorporated area of Rowland Heights , Grant said . She had been strangled . Hardin had worked as a security guard at the apartment complex . The district attorney 's office will seek the death penalty in the case since the murder allegedly occurred while Hardin was robbing the apartment , Grant said .
