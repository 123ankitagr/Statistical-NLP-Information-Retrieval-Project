METRO-Goldwyn-Mayer , the Hollywood studio taken over by Credit Lyonnais , its French bank creditor , reported a fourth-quarter net loss of Dollars 45.6m , compared with Dollars 114.2m in the same period of 1991 , and said the bank had agreed to support its business plan , which calls for substantial new film production , writes Martin Dickson in New York . MGM had an operating loss for the period of Dollars 8.5m , down from Dollars 65.3m in 1991 .
