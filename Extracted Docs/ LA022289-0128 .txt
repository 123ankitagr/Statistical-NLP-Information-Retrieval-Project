Worlds of Wonder Sold : The maker of Teddy Ruxpin talking teddy bears , which had lapsed into bankruptcy , has agreed to be sold to a group led by New York investor Eli Jacobs . Terms of the purchase of Worlds of Wonder Inc. , which will be in cash , debt and preferred stock , were not disclosed . The investor group plans to continue operating the company and Worlds will emerge from Chapter 11 upon court approval of the plan , it said . The Fremont , Calif. , toy maker filed for federal bankruptcy protection in December , 1987 .
