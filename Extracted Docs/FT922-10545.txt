channel 4 is likely to increase its share of advertising when it starts selling its own air time next year according to james capel the stockbrokers the itv companies presently sell channel 4's advertising air time and pay a subscription of 17 per cent of advertising revenue to fund both channel 4 and the welsh fourth channel s4c from next year the government will fund s4c which has had advertising revenues of about pounds 4 m a year mr neil blackley media analyst at james capel believes that next year channel 4 will be able to earn 17.6 per cent of terrestrial advertising revenue in its own right with the possibility of winning a greater share if it retains its audience
