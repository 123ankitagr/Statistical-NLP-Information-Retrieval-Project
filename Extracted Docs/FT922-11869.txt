the chicago board of trade the worlds largest futures exchange has signed a letter of intent to cooperate with knight ridder financial to develop an international commodity index future the index will be calculated and disseminated by the commodity research bureau crb a subsidiary of knightridder financial it will be productionweighted and consist of 18 commodity futures mr william oconnor cbot chairman said the exchange believed that the contract will appeal to institutional investors and commodity trading advisers around the world a crb index future currently trades on the new york futures exchange but it is weighted differently
