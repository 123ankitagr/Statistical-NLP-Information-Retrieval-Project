Forward Jonni Gray scored three 3-point baskets in the first half on her way to a 30-point game to lead Washington State past USC , 85-69 , Sunday in Pacific 10 Conference women 's basketball at Pullman , Wash. USC ( 6-11 overall , 2-5 in the Pac-10 ) outshot Washington State in the first half , 41 % to 40 % , but the Cougars ' consistency from the line ( 6 of 7 ) along with four 3-point baskets helped them take a 44-33 lead at halftime . Cherie Nelson and Teresa Strickland came off the bench to score 28 and 11 points , respectively , for USC . Angie Miller added 16 points for Washington State , ( 10-8 , 3-5 ) .
