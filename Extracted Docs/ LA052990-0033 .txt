question what are gallstones what are the symptoms that let you know that you have them what can be done to get rid of them answer gallstones are composed of cholesterol or calcium the symptoms include pain in the upper abdomen after a meal particularly one that includes fatty foods other symptoms include nausea vomiting and fever gallstones can be removed by surgery dissolved with drugs or treated by shock waves lithotripsy as many as 50 of all gallstones that are treated medically reappear within 10 years surgery is a permanent cure
