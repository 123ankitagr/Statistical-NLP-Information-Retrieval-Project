Ten sailors were killed yesterday in an explosion on board a French nuclear submarine off the Mediterranean port of Toulon , defence ministry officials said . The 2,400-tonne attack submarine Emeraude , of the Rubis class , was taking part in a training exercise . A navy spokesman , Captain Olivier Lajous , said the accident did not affect the submarine 's nuclear engine , it was was not carrying nuclear missiles and there was no risk of radiation . 'One of the two turbo alternators , which generate electrical power on board , exploded while the submarine was off the coast . We do n't know why , ' Capt Lajous said . The submarine was making its way to Toulon unassisted .
