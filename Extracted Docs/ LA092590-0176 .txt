The makers of Fuji film said today they will replace the plastic canisters in which their film is sold in the United States with paper ones as an environmental measure . In addition , Fuji Photo Film U.S.A. Inc. said most Fuji film boxes will be made from recycled paper . The new film canisters are made of moisture-proof paper that the Japanese company said it developed with its own technology . It said the containers provide the same protection as plastic ones .
