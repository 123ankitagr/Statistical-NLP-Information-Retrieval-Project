monday is christmas day and jan 1 is new year's day the following will be closed in observance of the holidays all county state and federal offices schools including colleges and universities public elementary junior and high schools will reopen jan 2 county state and federal courthouses city and county libraries banks and savings and loans city offices will be closed only on christmas and new year's days with the following exceptions fullerton offices will be closed monday through jan 1 those of buena park cypress garden grove la palma san juan capistrano and stanton will be closed monday and tuesday and jan 1 and 2 mail all post offices will be closed christmas and new year's days and mail deposited in mailboxes on those days will be picked up according to the posted holiday schedule trash there will be no trash pickup by city or county refuse workers christmas or new year's days trash pickups will be a day late for the rest of the week for both weeks buses orange county transit district buses will run on the holiday schedule christmas and new year's days call the district at 714 636-7433 for information the bus routes that will be running are 29, 41, 43, 44, 47, 49, 50, 51, 53, 54, 55, 57, 60, 64, 66, 69, 70 and 85 there will be no dial-a-ride or car-pool services available on the holidays parks all city and county-run parks and recreation facilities will be open during regular hours christmas and new year's days
