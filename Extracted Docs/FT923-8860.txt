Russian Andrei Perlov conquered heat , humidity and the steep climb to the Olympic stadium to take the men 's 50km road walk gold medal . Perlov , second in last year 's world championships , took an early lead and held on to win in an unofficial time of 3 hours 50.13 seconds . Italian Carlos Mercenario was second ( 3:52.09 ) and Germany 's Ronald Weigel , 1988 Olympic silver medallist , took bronze ( 3:53.45 ) .
