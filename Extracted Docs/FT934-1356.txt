Volkswagen said its supervisory board approved a restructuring plan for its loss-making Seat subsidiary in Spain after an extraordinary meeting on Saturday , Reuter reports from Berlin . Volkswagen said that it would spend about DM1.3bn ( Pounds 516m ) on measures at Seat by 1998 , including DM575m in 1993 . The company also plans a capital injection of DM1.5bn . Seat 's work force would be cut by about 9,000 from 22,400 .
