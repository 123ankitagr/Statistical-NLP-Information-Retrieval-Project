Larry Jinks , senior vice president-news and operations at Knight-Ridder , has been named publisher of the San Jose Mercury News , a Knight-Ridder newspaper . His successor will be announced later . Jinks will succeed William A. Ott , who will become chairman of the Mercury News , effective April . Jinks , 59 , is a former editor of the Mercury News and executive editor of the Miami Herald , another Knight-Ridder newspaper . He joined the corporate staff eight years ago as vice president-news . He became senior vice president-news in 1983 and assumed his current title in 1986 .
