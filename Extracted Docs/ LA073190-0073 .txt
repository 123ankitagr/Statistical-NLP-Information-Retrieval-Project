T-Bill Rates Mixed : The Treasury sold $ 9.3 billion of new three-month bills at an average discount rate of 7.50 % , up from 7.49 % last week . Another $ 9.2 billion in new six-month bills was sold at an average discount rate of 7.37 % , down from 7.40 % last week . The three-month bill rate was the highest since the 7.62 % rate of July 16 . The six-month bill rate was the lowest since averaging 7.30 % last Dec. 4 . The new discount rates understate the actual return to investors -- 7.75 % for three-month bills , with a $ 10,000 bill selling for $ 9,810.40 and 7.76 % for a six-month bill selling for $ 9,627.40 . The discount rate reflects the price discount received when government securities are purchased at less than face value .
