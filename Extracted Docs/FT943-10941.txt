Incentive , a leading industrial arm of the Sweden 's Wallenberg family , has begun restructuring its newly-acquired Cardo unit by announcing the sale of Bilsom to the French group Christian Dalloz , writes Christopher Brown-Humes in Stockholm . Bilsom , which has a SKr400m ( Dollars 51.5m ) annual turnover and 400 employees , is a leading supplier of protective equipment such as ear plugs , respiratory masks and safety glasses . The financial details were not disclosed . Incentive acquired control of Cardo in June after launching a SKr8.4bn bid for the group , which was formerly 44 per cent owned by Volvo . The deal expands the personal safety equipment business of Christian Dalloz , a quoted company with annual turnover of SKr650m .
