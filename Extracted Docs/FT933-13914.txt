The offer to intermediaries of shares in Quadramatic , the coin-handling and optical group being floated with a market value of Pounds 36.4m , was subscribed 2.48 times . Granville Davies , broker to the issue , said applicants for up to 20,000 shares would be fully satisfied , while those seeking more would receive about 34.8 per cent of the number applied for . Dealings will start on July 20 .
