The San Diego Area Dance Alliance has awarded $ 500 grants to support performances by four independent dance designers . Alison Cutri will team up with Rick Michalek for a program of solos at Three 's Company 's Lo-Tec Series . Kate Lounsbury will use her award for a concert at Sushi . Terri Sprague will take a program of `` Drum Dances '' to three local venues . And Pamela Turner will create an integrated theater piece in collaboration with San Diego composer Mary Kidd .
