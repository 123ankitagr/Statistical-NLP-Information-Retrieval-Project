The Southern California Water Co. , which serves 10 San Gabriel Valley cities , is moving its corporate headquarters to San Dimas . The new building , under construction west of San Dimas Canyon Road between Foothill Boulevard and Baseline Road , will cost $ 4.5 million . When the building is completed in early 1990 , 100 employees will move from the headquarters in Los Angeles . The company serves 255,000 customers in 61 cities throughout California , including Arcadia , Claremont , Covina , El Monte , La Verne , Monrovia , Pomona , Walnut , San Dimas and San Gabriel .
