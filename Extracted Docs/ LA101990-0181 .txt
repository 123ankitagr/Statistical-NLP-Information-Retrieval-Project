china today bought more than 43-million worth of u s wheat its second major subsidized purchase in two days the agriculture department announced the sale of 449,370 metric tons of winter wheat to china through the u s export subsidy program it came one day after china bought 299,750 tons of wheat also at discount prices china is one of the world's top customers for u s wheat buying 5.5 million tons in the 1989-90 marketing year half of its import total and 3.2 million tons so far this year which opened june 1 there are 36.7 bushels of wheat in a metric ton the chinese purchases this week worth more than 72 million based on chicago prices marked a welcome week of activity for lagging export sales the soviet union bought 350,000 tons of corn its first corn purchase since last may 15, and several smaller grain sales also were reported
