THE PRESIDENT and chief executive officer of Visa USA , Mr Robert Heller , has resigned saying he would pursue other interests , AP-DJ reports from San Francisco . Mr Heller , aged 53 , joined Visa in 1989 and was named president in 1991 . Mr Carl Pascarella , 50 , president of the company 's Asia Pacific division , succeeds Mr Heller . Visa is the world 's largest credit card company .
