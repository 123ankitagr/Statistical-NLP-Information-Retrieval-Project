SIR ROY Watts , chairman of Thames Water , has been missing since Tuesday afternoon , police said last night as they appealled for information on his whereabouts , Bronwen Maddox writes Sir Roy , 67 , - who is married with a son and two daughters , is understood to suffer from Parkinson 's disease . Scotland Yard said officers had found his wallet , with cash and credit cards , medication , and a pair of glasses in his Battersea flat in south London , but they did not know if they were his only pair . He did not appear to have extra clothes with him . Thames Water said Sir Roy had been at work as normal on Tuesday at Thames's central London headquarters . His driver took him home at 2pm , since when he had had no contact with the company or with his family in Oxford-shire .
