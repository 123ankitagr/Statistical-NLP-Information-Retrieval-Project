Management training for small and medium sized businesses in St Petersburg is to be provided by Manchester Business School over the next 12 months . The Pounds 700,000 programme , financed jointly by the UK government's Know-How Fund and the City of St Petersburg , will bring 120 owners and managers to Manchester during 1992 . The courses will include a period of project work with UK companies . Contact Peter Shepherd , Business Development Centre . Tel . 061 275 6544 .
