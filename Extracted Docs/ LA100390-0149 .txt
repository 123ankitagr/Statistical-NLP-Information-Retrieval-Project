FAX HOAX : The alert came via fax . President Ford 's pardon of Richard M. Nixon had been removed from its display case at the Richard Nixon Library in Yorba Linda . News organizations immediately called police to check out the report . . . . But John H. Taylor , the library 's executive director , knew instantly that the communique was a hoax . The library does not have Nixon 's pardon . After examining the fax , which had Gerald R. Ford 's middle initial wrong , Taylor said : `` It is a patent piece of fakery .
