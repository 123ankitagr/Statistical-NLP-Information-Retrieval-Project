Black & Decker Corp. , Towson , Md. , has named Nolan D. Archibald , president and chief executive , to the additional position of chairman , succeeding Peter L. Scott . Archibald was chairman from December , 1986 , until May , 1989 , when Black & Decker acquired Emhart Corp. Scott , formerly chairman of Emhart , was elected chairman of Black & Decker at that time . Scott had agreed to serve as chairman through the post-merger transition period but had indicated that he would not stand for reelection . Scott remains a director and a member of the executive committee .
