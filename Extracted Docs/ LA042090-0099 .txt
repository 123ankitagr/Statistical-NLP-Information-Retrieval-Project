A Glendale woman arrested after a hit-run accident had a blood-alcohol level more than five times the legal definition of drunk , authorities said Thursday . Cathylynne Marie Johnson , who weighs 105 pounds and had the equivalent of three quarters of a pint of bourbon in her system two hours after the accident , told investigating officers she did not drink , police said . She had a blood alcohol reading of .46 % , said Deputy Dist . Atty . Michael Pargament . In California , a reading of .08 % is considered proof of drunkenness .
