A summary of Southern California-related business litigation developments during the week of Dec. 18 . Movie Company Granted Restraining Order : Delta Unique Media Inc. was granted a temporary restraining order Tuesday in Los Angeles Superior Court to block First Cinema Venture Inc. from distributing the company 's adult films , including `` Naughty Nymphs , '' `` The Sinful Bed '' and `` Sorry , Wrong Bedroom . '' Judge Warren Deering ruled that First Cinema had distributed Delta Unique films without the company 's permission . The company , operated by a former Delta Unique employee , was ordered not to make any more distribution deals for Delta Unique films . No attorney for First Cinema appeared at the hearing , and the Santa Monica company could not be reached for comment . ( Filed Dec. 19 , 1989 , Case No . C747008 )
