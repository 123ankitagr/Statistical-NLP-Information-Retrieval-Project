Ukrainian President Leonid Kravchuk hopes a special aid package for his country 's energy industry can be approved at a Group of Seven industrial nations summit in Italy in July , the Spanish daily El Pais reported yesterday , Reuter reports from Madrid . 'It 's difficult to say what sum we are talking about but if we were given real aid of between Dollars 5bn ( Pounds 3.4bn ) and Dollars 8bn we would be able to manage the range of possibilities open to us quite well and not only in energy , ' Mr Kravchuk said .
