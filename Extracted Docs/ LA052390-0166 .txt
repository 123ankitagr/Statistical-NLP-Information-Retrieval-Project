Seven people suspected of belonging to a `` satanic music cult '' were arrested today in connection with the desecration of a Jewish cemetery in the town of Carpentras , police said . Police arrested 15 people in pre-dawn raids in the region around the town of Albi , 200 miles west of the southern town of Carpentras . All but the seven were released after identity checks , police said . Police said those held in the vandalism of 34 tombs May 10 are between 20 and 30 years old and are linked to a bizarre `` satanic music cult '' known as `` Trash . ''
