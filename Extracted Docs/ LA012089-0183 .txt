The Washington Huskies landed one football player , but lost another . Charles Mincey , a cornerback from Pasadena City College , has told the Huskies he plans to sign a letter of intent Feb. 8 to play football for Washington . But Glen Cavanaugh , a 6-foot-4 , 265-pound lineman from Gonzaga Prep High School in Spokane , said he will attend Stanford University , the Seattle Post-Intelligencer reported today . Washington and Washington State both recruited Cavanaugh . Mincey , who prepped at Los Angeles ' Dorsey High School , also was recruited by UCLA , USC , Pittsburgh and Iowa State . The Huskies rarely recruit junior college football players . They 've signed only six transfers , three of them kickers , in the last seven seasons .
