thyroid cancer among children in belarus rose steeply last year continuing a sharp upward trend since the 1986 chernobyl nuclear disaster in neighbouring ukraine a health ministry official said reuter reports from moscow mr valentin stezhko head of the ministry's chernobyl department said some 50 new cases were recorded in 1991 alone compared with 45 between 1986 and 1990 only seven cases of thyroid cancer were detected in the five years before the world's worst nuclear accident
