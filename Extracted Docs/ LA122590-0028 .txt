Players International Inc. in Calabasas , through a subsidiary , has entered into a joint venture with a Miami company to offer its call-in versions of `` Jeopardy ! '' and `` Wheel of Fortune '' to pay-phone customers and hotel guests . The games , accessible by dialing a 900 telephone number , are now offered only to residential and office telephone customers . Starting Jan. 1 , the games will also be accessible from more than 5,000 pay phones and up to 20,000 hotel rooms , said Doug Wise , executive vice president of Players Enterprises Inc. , the Players International subsidiary that operates the phone-in games . The program is being offered under a joint venture between Players and Peoples Telephone Inc. of Miami , an operator of pay telephones . Players International , an entertainment and travel promoter , offers discounts to gamblers at casino resorts .
