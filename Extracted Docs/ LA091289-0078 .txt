American Recreation Products Inc. , a subsidiary of Kellwood Co. , has completed its acquisition of Slumberjack Inc. of Los Angeles , company officials announced . The purchase price was not disclosed . American Recreation is an international manufacturer and marketer of tents , sleeping bags , hiking and climbing equipment under the Wenzel , Kelty and Ridgeway brands . Slumberjack is a manufacturer of sleeping bags and camping accessories under the Slumberjack and Everest Elite brands .
