Jonathan 's Acquisition : Fullerton-based Jonathan Manufacturing Corp. has acquired the aluminum slide division of Grant Hardware Co. of West Nyack , N.Y. No purchase price was disclosed . The division , formerly part of Grant Hardware 's operation in the City of Industry , will be moved to Fullerton . Grant Hardware will move another part of the City of Industry operation to Missouri , said William Wachtel , a spokesman for Jonathan Manufacturing . Jonathan Manufacturing designs and makes precision aluminum and steel slide mechanisms for the aerospace , military , industrial and commercial markets .
