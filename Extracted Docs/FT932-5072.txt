house prices in the uk fell by a seasonally adjusted 12 per cent in may according to the halifax house price index prices had risen 14 per cent in march and 17 per cent in april house prices are now 33 per cent below a year ago compared to last months figure of 23 per cent down on the year but figures from the nationwide building society this week showed a 1 per cent rise in house prices in may with prices down 55 per cent from a year ago the halifax said that the housing market showed clear signs of recovery but warned that realistic prices were still necessary to generate house sales the halifax predicts a gradual recovery in 1993 becoming more sustained in 1994 when house prices should grow broadly in line with earnings as a whole
