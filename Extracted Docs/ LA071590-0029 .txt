Jennifer Snow , Miss Bellflower of 1989-90 , will relinquish her crown at the 1990-91 Miss Bellflower Pageant scheduled Oct. 20 . The new Miss Bellflower will reign for one year and represent the city at various activities and functions . Prospective pageant entrants may register at Bellflower City Hall or call 804-1424 , Ext . 208 .
