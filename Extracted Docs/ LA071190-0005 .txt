The Times and staff writers Joel Sappell and Robert Welkos have done a commendable public service with the series on Hubbard and the Church of Scientology . While not as blatantly destructive as the Rev . Jim Jones ' Peoples Temple , the Church of Scientology is nevertheless a threat to American society . Scientology has tried to justify and protect itself by citing the constitutional guarantee of freedom of religion . Freedom of religion does not include the freedom to exploit , deceive or harass others . JERRY FALLENBERG Thousand Oaks
