Dozens of presumedly stolen bicycles will be displayed by Costa Mesa police Saturday in an attempt to reunite owners with their property . Police said 162 bikes recently confiscated in a police raid will be on exhibit from 8 a.m. until noon at the Costa Mesa city yard at 2310 Placentia Ave. A police spokesman said people who want to view the bikes must provide the name brand , color and serial number of their stolen bicycle . They added that any unclaimed bikes may be sold at a future date .
