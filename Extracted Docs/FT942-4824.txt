Mercury Communications , the main competitor to British Telecommunications , is to recruit 180 people for a new national directory inquiry centre in Glasgow , to supplement its centre in Birmingham . Mercury is already creating 350 jobs in Glasgow to provide other services . Mercury 's directory inquiry service is available only to Mercury subscribers . It provides all UK telephone numbers - including those of BT - and charges an initial 18p , after which customers pay by the second and may request as many numbers as they wish .
