arthur g newton an accountant and member of the city council for six years was elected mayor of placentia this week council members elected newton 40, by a 4-1 vote councilwoman maria moreno voted against newton after nominating john o tynes to the post tynes declined the nomination newton replaces norman z eckenrode whose term as mayor expired this year eckenrode was reelected to the city council in november tynes 74, was elected mayor pro tem he has served four terms on the city council since the 1950 s and is a retired superintendent of the placentia unified school district
