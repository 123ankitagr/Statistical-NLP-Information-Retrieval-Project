The men and women serving the country will get a good dose of country . . . music that is . The Marlboro Country Music tour is sponsoring three concerts on U.S. military bases , starting Memorial Day ( May 29 ) with the group Alabama and new country star Kathy Mattea at Camp Pendelton in Oceanside . Other shows will be held June 3 at Ft. Sill in Lawton , Okla. and June 4 at Ft. Carson in Colorado Springs , Colo . Proceeds from the Pendleton concert will benefit the base 's Morale , Welfare and Recreation Fund . Claudia Puig
