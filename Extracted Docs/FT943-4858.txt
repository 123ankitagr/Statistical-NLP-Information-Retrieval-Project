the docklands light railway in london is to resume evening services on october 3 for the first time in five years this follows the completion of the first stage of a pounds 46 m modernisation of its automatic train control system the railway which links the canary wharf office development with the city has shut in the evenings and at weekends to allow installation and testing of the new system weekend services will resume next spring the recently-opened eastern extension of the railway to beckton will remain closed in the evenings until mid 1995
