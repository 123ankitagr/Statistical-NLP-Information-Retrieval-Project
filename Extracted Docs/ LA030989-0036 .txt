The investor group that recently signed an agreement to acquire the assets of Worlds of Wonder Inc. , Fremont , has announced that it plans to name Joshua W. Denham president and chief executive of the company it will form to continue the operations of Worlds of Wonder . Denham was president of Mattel Electronics from 1979 to 1983 , and was most recently president of Wonder Products , a manufacturer of children 's products in Bosler City , La . After confirmation of the reorganization plan , expected in mid-May , Stephen J. Hopkins , who has been president and chief executive of the company since April , 1988 , will return to Connecticut-based Nightingale & Associates Inc. , a management consulting firm specializing in turning around financially troubled companies .
