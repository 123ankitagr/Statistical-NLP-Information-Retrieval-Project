christies in glasgow yesterday sold a run of rare chelsea football club programmes for pounds 22000 double the estimate the programmes were sold by mr david mears a descendant of the clubs founder such a complete run of programmes gathered into 69 bound volumes is unprecedented programmes dating from before 1914 are very rare and the collection included more than 400 of them plus more than 1000 pre1939 programmes the successful telephone bidder intends to keep the collection intact the auction did very well topping pounds 167000
