azusa pacific university has appointed the chairman of its board of trustees as interim president while it launches a nationwide search for a permanent president ted engstrom replaces paul e sago who resigned after nine years to become president of woodbury university in burbank engstrom is president emeritus of world vision an international christian relief organization he served as president of world vision from 198087 and was executive director and president of youth for christ founded in 1899 by the quakers azusa pacific is a liberal arts school affiliated with evangelical christian organizations about 1600 undergraduate students and 1100 graduates attend the school according to azusa pacific spokesman john carvalho
