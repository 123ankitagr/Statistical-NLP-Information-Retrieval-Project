Azusa Pacific University has appointed the chairman of its board of trustees as interim president while it launches a nationwide search for a permanent president . Ted Engstrom replaces Paul E. Sago , who resigned after nine years to become president of Woodbury University in Burbank . Engstrom is president emeritus of World Vision , an international Christian relief organization . He served as president of World Vision from 1980-87 and was executive director and president of Youth For Christ . Founded in 1899 by the Quakers , Azusa Pacific is a liberal arts school affiliated with evangelical Christian organizations . About 1,600 undergraduate students and 1,100 graduates attend the school , according to Azusa Pacific spokesman John Carvalho .
