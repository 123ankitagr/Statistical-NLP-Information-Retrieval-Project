salomon the us securities house has had about dollars 8 bn of outstanding debt downgraded by standard poor's the us credit rating agency the downgrade which could push up the cost of salomon's borrowings reflected the firm's reliance on proprietary trading activity which has increased its vulnerability to quarterly earnings volatility s p said salomon's longer-term senior debt rating was cut to single a minus from single a its subordinated debt rating fell to triple b plus from single a minus preferred stock received a rating of triple b from triple b plus and the commercial paper rating was cut to a-2 from a-1 mr donald howard chief financial officer said some rating agencies focus too heavily on the short-term volatility of salomon's earnings and give inadequate weight to our results measured over longer time-frames and to the exceptional quality and liquidity of our assets
