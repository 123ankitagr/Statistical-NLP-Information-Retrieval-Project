mci communications corp said it was awarded a nonexclusive threeyear contract by american express co to provide data messaging and voicenetwork services the contract is the largest awarded mci by american express since the two companies began working together in the early 1970s it includes an option for an additional twoyear period making a potential fiveyear arrangement valued at up to $ 100 million according to mci president bert c roberts jr the agreement calls for mci to provide services to all five american express operating units
