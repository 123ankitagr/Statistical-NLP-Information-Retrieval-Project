mci communications corp said it was awarded a non-exclusive three-year contract by american express co to provide data messaging and voice-network services the contract is the largest awarded mci by american express since the two companies began working together in the early 1970 s it includes an option for an additional two-year period making a potential five-year arrangement valued at up to 100 million according to mci president bert c roberts jr the agreement calls for mci to provide services to all five american express operating units
