MCI Communications Corp. said it was awarded a non-exclusive three-year contract by American Express Co. to provide data , messaging and voice-network services . The contract is the largest awarded MCI by American Express since the two companies began working together in the early 1970s . It includes an option for an additional two-year period , making a potential five-year arrangement valued at up to $ 100 million , according to MCI President Bert C. Roberts Jr . The agreement calls for MCI to provide services to all five American Express operating units .
