Meanwhile , Back at the Ranch : Michael Jackson led 82 children on a tour of his 3,000-acre ranch outside Santa Barbara on Saturday and later served them lunch . Childhelp USA , a nonprofit organization for the prevention of child abuse , sent residents of its village for abused and neglected children near Beaumont , Calif. , to Jackson 's ranch , where he keeps giraffes , llamas and other exotic beasts . Jackson donated the proceeds from his last concert in Los Angeles to Childhelp after it renamed its research facility the Michael Jackson International Research Institute . CLAUDIA PUIG
