ASK to Acquire Ingres for $ 110 Million : ASK Computer Systems , a Mountain View , Calif.-based supplier of manufacturing software , said it agreed to acquire Ingres Corp. , an Alameda-based software firm , for $ 9.25 per share in cash . Simultaneously , ASK announced that EDS Corp. and Hewlett-Packard have agreed to acquire 19.7 % and 10 % , respectively , of ASK common stock for an aggregate of $ 60 million in cash .
