Frank Lorenzo 's troubled Texas Air Corp. is discussing the sale of its computer reservation system to a General Motors Corp. unit in a deal that could bring the airline concern as much as $ 400 million , according to a report published today . Unidentified sources cited in the Wall Street Journal confirmed that negotiations with Electronic Data Systems Corp. were advanced and that barring a snag , an agreement could be forged within several weeks . Analysts estimated that Texas Air 's System One , the third-ranked computer reservation system in the airline industry , could fetch between $ 300 million and $ 400 million . Texas Air has given no indication of what it might do with the proceeds from any sale .
