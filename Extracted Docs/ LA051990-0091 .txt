Ryne Sandberg 's record streak of 123 games without an error ended Friday night with a wild throw against the Houston Astros . Sandberg , the Chicago Cubs ' Gold Glove second baseman , fielded a deflected ground ball by Eric Anthony in the fourth inning but threw wide of first baseman Mark Grace . Sandberg successfully handled 582 consecutive chances without an error . His last error had come June 20 against the Pittsburgh Pirates . Sandberg 's streak set a major league record for infielders , excluding first basemen .
