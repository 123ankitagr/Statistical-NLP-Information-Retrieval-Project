SEC Probing First Chicago Statements : The Securities and Exchange Commission has begun an inquiry into allegations that First Chicago Corp. leaked certain sensitive information to securities analysts in advance of a public announcement projecting lower first-quarter earnings . Some analysts said the probe has the potential to change the landscape of their profession . The SEC declined to comment on the inquiry . The analysts worry that allegations that First Chicago improperly disclosed an impending increase in non-performing loans could freeze the information flow that is their lifeblood . They said what they learned from First Chicago was the routine information that their profession relies on .
