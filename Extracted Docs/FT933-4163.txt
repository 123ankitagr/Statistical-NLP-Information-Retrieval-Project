the ec imposed permanent duties of 306 per cent on exports of allegedly underpriced chinese mountain bikes yesterday reuter reports from brussels the duties will be effective from saturday according to the ecs official journal replacing provisional duties of 344 per cent imposed in march local manufacturers complained that the chinese bikes were undermining their market the commission found that chinese prices undercut ec prices by a weighted 59 per cent the duties will be on all chinese bicycles not just the largetyre mountain variety
