construction has started on a 25-million town center in bonsall known as river village centruminvest a swedish company known for its shopping entertainment hospitality centers throughout northern europe is developing river village on 24 acres at mission road and california 76 overlooking the san luis rey river it is the firm's first project in the united states the center is being designed as a gathering place for residents of fallbrook bonsall and surrounding communities it will have 155,000 square feet of leasable space phase 1 is expected to be completed by next summer architects larson carpenter is project architect and nowell-thompson asla associates is the landscape architect ninetman construction co is general contractor and the neville co is project manager
