'Cafe ' Closes : `` Bagdad Cafe , '' the CBS comedy starring Whoopi Goldberg and Jean Stapleton , has gone out of production , CBS confirmed Monday . Sources close to the show say production shut down after a dispute between Goldberg and the show 's co-executive producer , Thad Mumford , during a Nov. 16 taping . A CBS spokeswoman said the network had not yet determined a date the show would go off the air . Goldberg 's representatives had no immediate comment on the matter . CLAUDIA PUIG
