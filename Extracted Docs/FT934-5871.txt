Complete lack of activity during the past fortnight has brought a weaker market for all grades of black and white pepper , reports Man Producten . In spite of the relatively low stocks everywhere more selling pressure has accumulated lately and prices started to drop faster , especially for white pepper . White muntok pepper is being offered at Dollars 2,800 a tonne , cif , and black pepper grade 1 can be bought at Dollars 1,400 , cif . White is certainly still overvalued whereas the opposite is true for black . November 26 - COMEX , CSCE , NYCE and NYMEX markets closed .
