WINTER World , a ski company with 65 groups of customers abroad , has ceased trading , the Association of British Travel Agents said last night . The company is an Abta member and clients ' money is protected . Abta said it did not yet know how many skiers were abroad or how many forward bookings the company had . The company , based in Skipton , North Yorkshire , refused to comment . The tour operator Riva Travel was placed in liquidation last night . The company , which employed failed travel entrepreneur Mr Harry Goodman , as a consultant , ceased trading last Wednesday . The liquidators are Mr Peter Souster and Mr Fred Satow of Baker Tilly .
