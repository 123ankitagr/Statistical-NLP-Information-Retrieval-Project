citroen part of the peugeot group of france is launching a new generation large family car in march to replace its 10yearold bx range writes kevin done motor industry correspondent the car will be named xantia a break from the recent citroen practice of naming cars with letters such as ax and zx it will follow french rival renault which has also switched to names such as clio and safrane the xantia pictured was styled in collaboration with bertone the italian design house
