Jobs Ads Down From a Year Ago : The volume of help-wanted job advertising in 51 of the nation 's major newspapers rose in July but was significantly below year-ago levels , the Conference Board reported . The board 's seasonally adjusted help-wanted advertising index rose 3 points to 149 in July after a three-month string of declines . About 63 % of the papers reported rising want-ad volume . In July , 1988 , the index was 160 and slightly more than 70 % of newspapers reported rising ad volume .
