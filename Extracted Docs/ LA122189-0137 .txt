a louisiana processor is voluntarily recalling about 1,000 pounds of a frozen pork and rice product because it might be contaminated with a food-poisoning organism listeria monocytogenes the agriculture department reported wednesday bodin foods inc of new iberia la sells the product through small distributors in arizona california florida louisiana and texas it is sold frozen in vacuum-sealed plastic packages with the name boo dan boudin link traditional cajun pudding on the label and est 8214 printed inside the usda inspection seal each 10-ounce package sold in groceries contains two links and is shipped 12 to a carton listeriosis was described as a rare but potentially serious disease most vulnerable are those with weakened immune systems including infants the elderly and the chronically ill symptoms include the sudden onset of flu-like symptoms such as fever chills headache respiratory distress backache loss of appetite and sometimes abdominal pain and diarrhea
