Time Warner Forms New Publishing Unit : In the first major consolidation since the $ 14-billion merger of Time Inc. and Warner Communications Inc. , Time Warner Inc. said it was forming a unit comprising Little , Brown & Co. and Warner Books . Annual revenue for the combined entity , to be called Time Warner Trade Publishing and operated by Time Inc. Book Co. , should exceed $ 160 million , the company said . Both houses are to continue publishing with separate imprints . Little Brown is a 152-year-old , hard-cover and professional publishing house , which was controlled by Time , and Warner Books was Warner Communications .
