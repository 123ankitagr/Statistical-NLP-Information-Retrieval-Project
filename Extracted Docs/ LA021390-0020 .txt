Viva la Raza ! For the first time , Mexican movie makers are honoring their Chicano counterparts by showing Chicano feature films and videos and opening a dialogue with them through a series of informal meetings and workshops that continue through Thursday . Luis Valdez , director-writer of `` La Bamba , '' and `` Zoot Suit , '' will be honored by Mexican President Carlos Salinas de Gortari who will present him with an award . Other Chicano film makers and artists attending the event are Moctesuma Esparza , producer of `` Milagro Beanfield War , '' rock guitarist Carlos Santana and Gregory Nava , producer of `` El Norte .
