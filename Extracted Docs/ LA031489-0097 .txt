Insurer Ordered to Stop : The California Department of Insurance ordered First Farwest Life Insurance Co. of Portland , Ore. , to cease transacting insurance business in California . The firm , which was placed in receivership Feb. 27 by the Oregon Department of Insurance , is threatened with insolvency and operating in a hazardous manner , the California department said . The firm wrote slightly less than $ 100 million in life , accident and health insurance in California in the first nine months of 1988 .
