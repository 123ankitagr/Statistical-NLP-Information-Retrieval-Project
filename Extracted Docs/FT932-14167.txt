Landed demand continued good but selective , reports the Tea Broker's Association . Brightest liquoring East African teas remained fully firm to dearer but all other descriptions were irregular and generally 3 to 5p easier particularly towards the close . Offshore good demand but prices were barely steady . The highest price realised this week was 195p for a Rwanda pf . 1 . Quotations quality 160p/kg , nom good medium 125p/kg , medium 115p/kg , low medium 90p/kg .
