An elderly man was found dead Tuesday morning in the bedroom of his East San Diego home , his hands tied behind his back , police said . The dead man was identified as Romie Hall , 84 , a retired security guard . The cause of death and motive for the apparent homicide are unknown . An autopsy will be performed today , a spokeswoman for the San Diego County coroner said . Police said neighbors became concerned when they discovered Hall 's front door ajar and his car missing Monday evening , although no one called 911 until Tuesday morning . When officers arrived at 9:43 a.m. , they found Hall lying face down in a bedroom . The burned-out shell of Hall 's car was found Tuesday night by San Diego police .
