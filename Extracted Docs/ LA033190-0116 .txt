Paltrow Near Columbia Deal : Bruce Paltrow , the former executive producer of `` St . Elsewhere , '' is in final stages of negotiation to move his independent production company , Paltrow Group , to Columbia Pictures Television . Paltrow , who is making a summer series for CBS and has fall pilots there and at NBC , has been without a studio deal since ties were severed with MTM Entertainment last year . Scott Siegler , president of Columbia Pictures TV , was unavailable for comment .
