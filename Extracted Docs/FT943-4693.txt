russia's private farmers are expected to harvest about 10 m tonnes of grain this year despite a shortage of fuel and agricultural equipment the head of akkor the private farmers association said on tuesday reuter reports from moscow for the first time since 1917, private farmers will export grain to foreign countries mr vladimir bashmachnikov said private farms produced about 5 m tonnes of grain last year out of total output of 99 m tonnes officials have predicted a total grain harvest of 90m-100m tonnes this year
