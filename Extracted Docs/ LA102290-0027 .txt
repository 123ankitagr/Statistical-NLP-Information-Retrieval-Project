Freshman Joe-Max Moore 's goal in the seventh minute of overtime Sunday sparked third-ranked UCLA to a 2-0 victory over Wake Forest for the championship of the sixth annual UCLA-Met Life Classic soccer tournament . The shot by midfielder Moore from 15 yards in front of the goal came after the teams were scoreless in regulation . Freshman Sean Henderson 's header in the 100th minute provided the final goal . UCLA improved to 13-1-3 . Wake Forest fell to 8-6-2 . Moore , who had three assists in the Bruins ' 3-0 victory over Air Force Friday night , was named the tournament 's offensive most valuable player . Wake Forest 's Craig Conger was defensive MVP .
