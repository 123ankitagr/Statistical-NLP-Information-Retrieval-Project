soviet president mikhail s gorbachev has canceled a planned visit to oslo for the presentation of his nobel peace prize tass news agency said today tass quoted the soviet presidents press office as saying plans to attend the dec 10 presentation were changed because of the crucial situation in the country and the presidents preoccupation with affairs that occupy his constant attention gorbachev awarded the peace prize for his reforms that allowed the end of the cold war and the flourishing of democratic movements in eastern europe has been criticized at home for devoting too much time to foreign affairs and overseas trips while the soviet union suffers serious shortages and domestic problems it is hoped in moscow that the nobel committee will understand the reason for this postponement and agree to present the prize at a later date tass said
