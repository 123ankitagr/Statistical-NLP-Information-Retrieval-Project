TV Movie Set on Sex Video Case : A 23-year-old woman who was awarded a $ 1.1-million judgment after she was secretly videotaped having sex has reached an agreement with a producer to film a television movie about the case . Attorneys for Susan Leigh Kerr said Tuesday that a NBC Monday Night Movie will be made by Los Angeles producer Joan Barnett . Kerr , a senior at Texas Tech University , won the judgment and attracted national attention last June after she filed suit against her former boyfriend , Dan Boyles Jr. , and four other men who secretly taped her having sex with Boyles . The tape later was played for Boyle 's college fraternity members . CLAUDIA PUIG
