incentive the swedish industrial group controlled by the wallenberg family is set to become the world's leading supplier of shipboard cargo handling equipment after agreeing to buy macgregor navire from kone the finnish elevators group the acquisition will strengthen the position of incentive's materials handling company hagglunds marine and create a unit with annual sales of more than skr2bn dollars 245 m payment is in the region of skr500m although exact details are not being disclosed hagglunds is the world's leading supplier of shipboard cranes acquiring macgregor will expand its product range to include hatch covers marine elevators roll-on roll-off equipment and cargo lashing equipment it will have a market share of between 50 and 60 per cent according to mr mikael lilius incentive president mr lilius said we think it is a good time to buy as the order books of both companies are growing and they are benefiting from the devaluation of the finnish and swedish currencies he added that macgregor-navire's international network of 50 offices in 24 countries would greatly enhance hagglund's own sales and service network this is incentive's most significant purchase since it gained a stock market listing in 1991 in two years of rationalisation it has raised dollars 400 m from selling businesses and cut net debt by skr3bn
