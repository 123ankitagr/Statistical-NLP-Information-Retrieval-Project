borden inc said today it has agreed to acquire fiap group of milan italy in a move that will increase borden's european plastic packaging business by about 60 borden a diversified consumer products company that last month announced 7,000 layoffs and the closure of 65 dairy plants did not disclose how much it will pay for fiap fiap's estimated 1989 sales are about 90 million compared with about 150 million for borden's existing european plastic films and packaging units borden the nation's largest dairy company also is a leading manufacturer of plastic food wraps used by institutions and restaurants
