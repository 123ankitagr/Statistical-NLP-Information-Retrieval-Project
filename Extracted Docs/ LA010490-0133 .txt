Vice President Dan Quayle will attend the Jan. 27 inauguration of the new president of Honduras and will visit Europe next month , his spokesman said Wednesday . Quayle 's schedule includes a one-day visit to Honduras for the inauguration of Rafael Leonardo Callejas , elected in November , said Quayle 's press secretary , David Beckwith . Sources said the possibility of additional stops in the region -- such as Panama or El Salvador -- are under consideration . The vice president will be in Europe from Feb. 4-11 , with stops planned at North Atlantic Treaty Organization headquarters in Brussels , Geneva , London and Bonn and Munich , West Germany . Quayle may also visit Berlin and Czechoslovakia . `` He wants to consult with our allies on U.S.-European issues of the '90s , '' Beckwith said .
