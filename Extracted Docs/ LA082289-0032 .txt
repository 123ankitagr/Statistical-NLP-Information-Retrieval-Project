A San Diego man , accused of killing a man he had just met , was ordered Monday to stand trial on a murder charge . Ernest Eugene Scott , 31 , of East San Diego is accused of fatally shooting Antonio Silva , 33 , on July 26 in the garage of Silva 's home in on Mt . Herbert Avenue in Clairemont . Scott , who is being held in lieu of $ 1-million bail , shot Silva during an argument , Deputy Dist . Atty . David A. Stutz said . Testimony during Monday 's preliminary hearing indicated that Scott was under the influence of methamphetamine . Scott will be arraigned in Superior Court on Sept. 1 .
