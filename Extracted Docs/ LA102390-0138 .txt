Kip Parsons and Neal Brockmeier each had three goals to lead Villa Park High School , the sixth-ranked water polo team in Orange County , to a 12-8 victory over fourth-ranked El Toro in a nonleague game Monday at Villa Park . Villa Park ( 16-6 ) led , 5-4 , at halftime and outscored El Toro ( 16-6 ) 4-0 in the third quarter to take control . Robert Bowman had 11 saves for Villa Park .
