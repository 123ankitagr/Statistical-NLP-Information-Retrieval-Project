The 19-year-old son of Mayor Maynard Jackson apologized for knocking down his girlfriend on a street , but it was n't enough to keep him out of jail . Municipal Judge Harvey Moskowitz on Monday upheld a charge of disorderly conduct and ordered Maynard Jackson III to complete a 45-day sentence . Jackson has been in jail since soon after the June 18 incident involving Maria Norma Duarte , 30 . He was charged with pushing her to the ground after trying to grab her can of Mace . It was his third arrest this year . `` I know I was wrong , '' he told the judge . `` I should have held back my temper . ''
