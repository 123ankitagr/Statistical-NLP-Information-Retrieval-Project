First Citizens Bank in Sherman Oaks said its net income for the fourth quarter that ended Dec. 31 fell 37 % to $ 27,394 from $ 43,527 a year earlier , when it paid no taxes because of a tax credit . Pretax income more than doubled to $ 109,078 for the period . First Citizens ' total assets increased about 18 % to $ 32.5 million from $ 27.6 million . For the year that ended Dec. 31 , First Citizens ' net income increased 61 % to $ 403,890 from $ 250,630 in 1988 .
