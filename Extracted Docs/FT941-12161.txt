robert clements has been named chairman and chief executive of marsh mclennan risk capital a wholly owned subsidiary of marsh mclennan companies the new yorkbased company which is the worlds biggest insurance broker and insurance services group clements 61 is regarded as one of the most innovative thinkers in the international insurance industry and has been associated with marsh mclennans backing for new reinsurance ventures in bermuda such as ace exel and midocean he joined marsh in 1959 and has for many years headed the groups worldwide insurance broking operations he has been president of the parent company since 1992 a position he will now relinquish marsh mclennan risk capital was formed in 1992 to manage and develop marsh mclennans insurance marketmaking activities clements says it will invest in startup operations in the industry and make friendly investments in existing insurance and reinsurance ventures where it believes it can increase shareholder value
