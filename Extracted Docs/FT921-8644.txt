yugoslav vice-president branko kostic has said he would never allow the yugoslav army to withdraw from the republic of bosnia-herzegovina while he remained a member of the federal presidency reuter reports from belgrade his comments will alarm the ethnically-mixed republic which is due to hold a referendum on independence on february 29 and march 1 bosnia-herzegovina has a mixed population of moslems serbs and croats its politicians fear that fighting between croat militias and serb irregulars backed by the yugoslav army could spill over into their republic
