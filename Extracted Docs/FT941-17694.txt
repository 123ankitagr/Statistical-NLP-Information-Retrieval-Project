The transformation of the new issue market over the past 12 months took the City of London by surprise . At this time last year financiers were worried that the continuing recession and what looked to be high government borrowing would leave room for only a handful of companies to seek a public listing . In the event , the year 's 165 flotations - a five-year high - raising Pounds 5.1bn in new capital assuaged these fears but created their own difficulties . There have been doubts about the quality of some stocks as the high stock market became a more lucrative option than trade sales .
