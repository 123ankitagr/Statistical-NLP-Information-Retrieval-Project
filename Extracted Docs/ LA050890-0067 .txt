Live Entertainment Inc. , a Van Nuys-based home video and recorded music distributor , said it completed the acquisition of the majority of Munich-based VCL Communications GmbH , a German home video distributor , for an undisclosed price . Live said the acquisition of VCL , which has annual revenue of more than $ 25 million , marks its debut in overseas home video distribution . Live said VCL would give it a foothold in Europe in advance of the integration of the European Common Market in 1992 . VCL is expected to acquire the rights for German-speaking areas to many of the films produced by Carolco Pictures Inc. Carolco owns a 49 % voting interest in Live .
