Several leading providers are reducing their rates while others , which have been uncompetitive , are putting them up . One reason for the decrease is that the long-term gilts yield has again drifted lower , to 8.87 on October 12 after a high of 9.25 on September 20 . Generali , one of the large players , increased its rates by roughly 5.7 per cent on October 4 . But Canada Life , Sun Life of Canada and Prudential cut theirs this week .
