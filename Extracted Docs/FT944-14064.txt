Calling all Internet freaks . Latest addition to the Internet is an interactive tour of the White House . Click on a computerised version of the Clintons ' cat Socks and hear a meow . Click on President Clinton and he says : 'This is the very first on-line tour of the White House . ' Vice president Al Gore plays a starring role . Users can click to see the vice president 's favourite political cartoons or read about his accomplishments . Another click of the mouse and the computer announces that Gore is ' a leader on science , space and technology ' . A veritable Pied Piper , indeed .
