Sheldon S. King , president and chief executive officer of Stanford University Hospital , will resign effective May 1 to become president of Cedars-Sinai Medical Center in Los Angeles , a spokesman said . King , 57 , has been at Stanford since 1981 . `` He is going to a major metropolitan center , '' hospital spokesman Spyros Andreopoulos said . `` He has been here long enough to do what he wanted to do . '' Among King 's objectives was to open a modernized wing of the Stanford Hospital . After a decade of planning , the $ 153-million wing opened to patients two weeks ago .
