A 38-year-old woman was injured Wednesday morning on Interstate 5 when her car collided with a school bus while changing lanes , the California Highway Patrol reported . The motorist , Lilia Ciballos , of San Diego was northbound on the freeway , just south of Interstate 8 , when her car was hit on the left side by the bus . She was taken to UC San Diego Medical Center , where she underwent surgery for treatment of leg injuries . None of the children on the bus were injuried .
