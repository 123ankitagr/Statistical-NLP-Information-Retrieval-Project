ATTITUDES to sexual harassment depend more on age than gender , according to a Mori poll of nearly 2,000 adults . Men over the age of 55 were more likely than younger males to blame women for dressing provocatively . The GMB general union , which commissioned the poll , said it was surprised by the results . 'We expected men and women to have different attitudes . . . What we found was that the sexes agreed but the generations differed . '
