Mitch Evans scored his first win in unlimited hydroplane racing by driving Cooper 's Express to the Columbia Cup championship over Miss Budweiser at Kennewick , Wash. Evans , 28 , was battling Mike Hanson , in Miss Mazda , for second place on the fourth of five laps when Tom D'Eath , in Miss Budweiser , cut in front of Miss Mazda . The spray temporarily knocked out Miss Mazda 's engine and resulted in a one-lap penalty for Miss Budweiser . Cooper 's Express averaged 113.531 m.p.h . in the 10-mile race on the Columbia River . Miss Mazda was second , Miss Budweiser third .
