The new Miss World is America 's Gina Tolleson , who was judged more beautiful than 80 other women from around the world despite a bout with influenza . Miss Ireland , Siobhan McClafferty , 20 , came in second , and Miss Venezuela , Sharon Gonzalez , 19 , was third in the contest Thursday evening in the London Palladium theater . `` I felt so bad during dress rehearsals that I had to get off stage , and all I could do was pray I would be OK , '' Tolleson said about her flu , which apparently was caused by London 's legendary inclement weather .
