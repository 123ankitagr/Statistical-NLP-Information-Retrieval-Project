france has told belgium that western european union weu members will be invited to join a francogerman military force to make it the core of european defence according to belgiums deputy prime minister mr willy claes reuter reports from paris he was speaking after meeting president francois mitterrand mr mitterrand and chancellor helmut kohl announced last year plans to set up a joint 35000strong corps by 1995 as a model for an ec defence arm separate from the north atlantic treaty organisation they have already commissioned a 5000strong francogerman brigade and will launch a joint naval squadron next month
