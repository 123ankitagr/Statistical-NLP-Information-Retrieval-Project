DELTA AIRLINES has applied for authority to fly between Manchester and New York 's Kennedy Airport , a route operated by British Airways and American Airlines . Earlier this month Continental , another US carrier , applied for a licence for the route from Manchester to Newark , New Jersey , a route Virgin Atlantic is considering applying for . Manchester Airport said yesterday that four other new routes were under serious consideration by US carriers . American , which flies daily from Manchester to Chicago and New York , is looking at Miami and Dallas as new destinations , while United Airlines is considering Washington , and Northwest Airlines has its eye on Detroit .
