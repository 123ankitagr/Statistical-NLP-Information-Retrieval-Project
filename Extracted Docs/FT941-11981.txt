Westfield Trust , Australia 's largest listed property trust , yesterday announced plans to raise ADollars 285m ( USDollars 202m ) , via a one-for-seven rights issue , writes Nikki Tait in Sydney . The issue price for the new units is pitched at ADollars 2.50 , compared with Monday 's closing price of ADollars 2.78 . Westfield said that the funds would be used to finance the expansion and redevelopment of Westfield shopping centres .
