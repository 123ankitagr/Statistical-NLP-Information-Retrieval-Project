mayor ernie kell has reappointed one member of the long beach harbor commission and extended the term of another keeping the fivemember panel intact for at least another year kell announced this week that he would not replace commissioner louise duvall an attorney citing the need to maintain the direction and stability of the port instead she will remain in her spot for about another year before any change is considered again kell said duvall who has been on the commission since 1979 will serve as its president this fiscal year the city council this week approved the mayors reappointment of david l hauser a real estate agent to a second sixyear term on the commission the city council also gave the goahead to new appointments to the citys redevelopment agency board and planning commission physician judson r schoendorf and james r kennelly were appointed to fouryear terms on the redevelopment agency board which has five members they replace thomas e stewart whose second term ended in may and msgr ernest gualderon whose second term officially ended in 1987 but was extended another two years dentist ebenezer bush will serve a fouryear term on the sevenmember planning commission he replaces dr horace rains who served two terms
