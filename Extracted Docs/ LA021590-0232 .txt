Reebok International Ltd. said today it had combined its domestic and international Reebok divisions , and the president of the U.S. operation announced his resignation . Frank O'Connell , who was president of the U.S. division the past two years , said he is departing the athletic-shoe company `` with positive feelings . '' `` It was a situation where I did n't see the opportunity to exercise my leadership skills , '' O'Connell said . John Duerden , president of the international division , will be president of the combined business .
