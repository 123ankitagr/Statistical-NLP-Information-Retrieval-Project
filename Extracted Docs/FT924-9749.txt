BRITISH STEEL is considering extending Christmas holidays in its strip division to reduce production . It could not say how many workers faced short-time working and extended Christmas holidays . Market conditions were 'changing all the time ' and not all workers were likely to be affected in the same way . The corporation said that it would not rule out redundancies although it emphasised that no final decision had been taken . It would not to disclose how many of its 3,000 workers at the Llanwern strip production plant were working 15 shifts a week rather than the usual 21 .
