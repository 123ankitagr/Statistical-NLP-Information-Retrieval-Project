Iraqi Gen. Adnan Khairallah , defense minister for 10 years and President Saddam Hussein 's closest confidant , was buried in a state funeral after he was killed in a helicopter crash in northern Iraq . In a statement broadcast by the state radio and television , the Iraqi leader said the pilot of the helicopter flying the 50-year-old general to Baghdad on Friday lost control during a sandstorm . One person reportedly survived the crash . Khairallah , deputy commander in chief of the armed forces , had been considered the second-most powerful official in the tight-knit clan that rules the country . He was both a cousin and brother-in-law to Hussein .
