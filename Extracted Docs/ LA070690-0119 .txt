SUCH A DEAL : You can get souvenirs of dedication day ( July 19 ) and opening day ( July 20 ) at the Nixon Library for only 25 cents each , the cost of a first-class stamp . . . . On those days , the library 's temporary post office will stamp a commemorative postmark on mail brought to it . . . . Or have the same postmark mailed to you . Send a self-addressed , stamped envelope to Postmaster , Richard Nixon Library and Birthplace , Yorba Linda , Calif. 92686-9998 , and specify which day 's postmark you want . Mark I.
