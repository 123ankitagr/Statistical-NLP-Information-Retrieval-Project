Accident insurance is being launched by Lloyds bank to provide a lump sum for injury , disability or death . The policy will also pay out for loss of a limb or loss of sight or hearing . Premiums are between Pounds 5.95 to Pounds 9.25 a month . The two levels of cover provide a lump sum disability payment of Pounds 60,000 or Pounds 100,000 . Insurers adapt to different lives , Page VI
