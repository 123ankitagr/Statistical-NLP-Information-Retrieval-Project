Randy Lewis ' feature on Dodger Stadium organist Nancy Hefley ( `` Career Takes Her Out to Ballgame a Lot , '' Calendar , Sept. 24 ) was a welcome piece of light reading . It was almost as relentlessly upbeat as the cheerful lady in the Dodgers ' music chair , but one piece of history must be corrected : Ms. Hefley is the fifth person to hold the key position since the Dodgers moved west , after Bob Mitchell , Chauncey Haines , Don Beamsley and Helen ( the Iron Woman ) Dell , who is without question the Lou Gehrig of ballpark organists . RUSSELL KISHI , Glendale
