Nobody won the $ 4.8-million jackpot in the latest `` Lotto 6-53 '' game , and lottery officials said today that Wednesday 's prize could reach $ 9 million . Because no player picked all six numbers , the $ 4.8-million prize will roll over to Wednesday night 's drawing . Lottery officials expected that six-of-six jackpot to reach $ 9 million . There were five tickets with five numbers plus the bonus number that won $ 110,611 apiece . The tickets were purchased in Burbank , Hawthorne , Los Angeles and two in Daly City .
