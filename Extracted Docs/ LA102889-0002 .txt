How can human beings guzzle beer , cheer wildly , do high-fives and celebrate five miles away from a scene where they were digging for bodies of earthquake victims ? Perhaps the loss of huge revenues by the networks , advertisers , media , fans et al is more precious to some than the human lives and misery that prevails at this time . You wonder how far we have come since Nero fiddled while Rome burned . BLYDEN BOYLE , Pacific Palisades
