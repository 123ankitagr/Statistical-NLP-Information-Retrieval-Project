The Dream Doctor earned his fourth win in five lifetime starts when he posted the fastest qualifying time for the estimated $ 75,000 California Sires Cup Derby on Wednesday night at Los Alamitos . The 10 fastest horses from Wednesday 's trials will return for the final Nov. 10 . The Dream Doctor won the first of two trials for the 400-yard stake for 3-year-old California-breds . The gelded son of Merridoc covered the 400 yards in 20.01 seconds while defeating Here Comes the Band , which had the second fastest qualifying time of 20.10 seconds .
