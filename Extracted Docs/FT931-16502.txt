SOUTH KOREAN companies will acquire an industrial complex in Qasim Port , near Karachi , this year , Mr Nawaz Sharif , the Pakistani prime minister , said yesterday in a meeting with the chairman of Daewoo , the South Korean conglomerate , in Islamabad , writes John Burton in Seoul . Mr Kim Woo-choong , the Daewoo chairman , said there would be a ban on trade union activity at the industrial park , which will be leased by Daewoo . He added that 17 Korean companies were interested in the industrial complex and investment could total Dollars 400m .
