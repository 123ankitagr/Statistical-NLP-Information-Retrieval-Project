New Name for Rice Brand : The Rice Growers Assn . of California has nearly three months to come up with a new name for its Hinode California Harvest brand . The Hinode California Harvest brand name for long-grain rice will disappear from grocery shelves by Sept. 25 under an agreement reached in a trademark infringement case . The agreement , subject to approval by a federal judge , will end legal action by Pacific International Rice Mills Inc. of Woodland against the association , a grower-owned co-op .
