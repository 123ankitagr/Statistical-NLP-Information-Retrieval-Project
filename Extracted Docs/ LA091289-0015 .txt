House of Fabrics , a Sherman Oaks-based chain of fabric and sewing stores , lost $ 119,000 in its fiscal second quarter that ended July 31 , contrasted with a $ 454,000 profit a year earlier . The company 's sales rose 13 % to $ 81.3 million from $ 72 million . But House of Fabrics , which operates 644 stores , said the sales growth was not as strong as it had hoped in light of the company 's increased use of advertising and promotions during the quarter . For the first half of its fiscal year , House of Fabrics ' profit fell 50 % , to $ 1.22 million from $ 2.46 million . Its six-month sales rose 9 % to $ 160.8 million from $ 147 million .
