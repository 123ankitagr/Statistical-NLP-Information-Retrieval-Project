country singer naomi judd announced wednesday in nashville that she will retire early next year for health reasons the announcement signals the end of the judds the mother and daughter team that has been one of country music's most successful attractions since 1984 judd 44, who was found to have hepatitis 10 months ago said the retirement will come at the end the duo's current love can build a bridge tour wynonna her 26-year-old daughter plans to continue in country music as a solo artist the judds who have had more than a dozen no 1 country hits including why not me and grandpa tell me bout the good old days have been honored by the country music assn as the field's best vocal group duo for the past six years
