out-of-work managers over age 50 found jobs faster than their younger colleagues in the job market last summer according to a survey released today by challenger gray christmas inc the results represent the first time in 15 years of tracking job searches that the 50-and-over age group has surpassed its younger counterparts in the length of time it takes to find a new job said james e challenger president and founder of the job-search consulting firm the firm's survey of third-quarter employment patterns showed the median time needed for an out-of-work manager age 50 or over to find new work was 3.23 months unemployed managers in all other age groups required a median time of 3.26 months
