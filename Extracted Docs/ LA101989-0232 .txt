outofwork managers over age 50 found jobs faster than their younger colleagues in the job market last summer according to a survey released today by challenger gray christmas inc the results represent the first time in 15 years of tracking job searches that the 50andover age group has surpassed its younger counterparts in the length of time it takes to find a new job said james e challenger president and founder of the jobsearch consulting firm the firms survey of thirdquarter employment patterns showed the median time needed for an outofwork manager age 50 or over to find new work was 323 months unemployed managers in all other age groups required a median time of 326 months
