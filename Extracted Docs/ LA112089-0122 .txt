Entertainer Michael Jackson has been hanging out the last couple of weeks at Steve Wynn 's new $ 630-million Mirage Hotel , which opens Wednesday on the Las Vegas Strip . Jackson and Wynn are longtime friends . Jackson has popped up at interviews Wynn has conducted with reporters checking out his new 3,100-room showplace . One reporter from an Eastern newspaper had to be convinced by Wynn that Jackson was , in fact , THE Michael Jackson and not a Las Vegas look-alike . Jackson and Wynn are planning a special feature at the resort that will be known as the `` Jackson Attraction , '' an area featuring Jackson memorabilia and souvenirs .
