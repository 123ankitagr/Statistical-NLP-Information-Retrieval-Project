The mayor of South Lake Tahoe , his wife and 16 other people have been arrested in connection with a 20-month undercover drug and money-laundering investigation , authorities said . Mayor Terry Trupp and his wife , Kim , were taken into custody on federal conspiracy and money-laundering charges involving alleged efforts to launder about $ 750,000 to FBI and Internal Revenue Service agents , officials said . The Trupps , who are being held without bail in El Dorado County Jail , will be arraigned today before a federal magistrate . Besides the 18 arrested in the probe , dubbed `` Deep Snow , '' authorities recovered about 12 kilograms of cocaine in arrests and seizures , agents said . No drugs were seized in the Trupps ' arrests , investigators said .
