Mortgage Rates at Year 's Peak : Fixed-rate home mortgages are currently averaging 10.67 % , their highest rate in nearly a year , according to a national survey released by the Federal Home Loan Mortgage Corp . It was the highest rate since fixed-rate mortgages averaged 10.69 % last May 19 . They averaged 10.56 % last week . On one-year adjustable-rate mortgages , lenders asked an average initial rate of 8.62 % last week , up from 8.56 % the week before .
