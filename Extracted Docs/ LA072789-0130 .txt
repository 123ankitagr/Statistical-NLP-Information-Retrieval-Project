parents of children with cancer have organized a softball tournament at yorba regional park this weekend to raise money for their support group parent-organizers tom and patricia mcdermott and jeff and donna maloney are hoping to raise about 6,000 this year for the uci cancer center parent support group through the tournament on saturday and sunday the cost per co-ed team is 200 and the mcdermotts and maloneys are hoping 20 teams will play for registration information call 714 528-3679 the mcdermotts have a 3-year-old daughter with leukemia the maloneys son jeff died earlier this year from burkitt's lymphoma
