a clash between moscow and the breakaway southern russian republic of chechnya was averted yesterday when russian troops withdrew from a disputed area on chechnyas borders writes our foreign staff the troops sent into neighbouring ingushetia to quell a fierce outbreak of ethnic violence had occupied three regions claimed by the chechen republic which declared independence from russia last year they were withdrawn following allnight negotiations between the two sides and mr yegor gaidar the acting russian prime minister more than 200 people have died in fighting between ingush and ossetians from the neighbouring russian republic of north ossetia over the past week according to russian television
