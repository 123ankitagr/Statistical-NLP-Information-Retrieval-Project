North Hollywood 24 , Grant 14 -- Leon Gable rushed for 221 yards and three touchdowns to lead North Hollywood at Grant ( 3-6 , 1-5 ) . It was an outing that Husky fans have become accustomed to : Gable leads City Section players in the Valley with 1,639 rushing yards . North Hollywood ( 5-3 , 4-2 in league play ) gave the ball to Gable on 42 of its 57 plays . Gable is closing in on the school single-season record of 1,900 rushing yards , set by Chip Grant in 1987 .
