imports of cattle from the czech and slovak republics were banned by the ministry of agriculture yesterday after positive tests for foot and mouth disease the ban also applies to pigs deer sheep goats and other susceptible clovenhoofed animals there have been 51 outbreaks of the disease in italy this year with eastern europe suspected as the source and thousands of animals have been slaughtered the ministry said the positive tests did not necessarily indicate that animals were infected as those vaccinated against the disease showed a similar reaction
