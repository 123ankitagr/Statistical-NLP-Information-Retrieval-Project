Talking Heads : `` Mikey 's Back ! With the voice of Bruce Willis , '' announces an ad in Variety for `` Look Who 's Talking Too '' the sequel to last fall 's hit by Amy Heckerling . But this time , Mikey will be joined by some toddler friends , whose voices will also sound familiar . Roseanne Barr will be the voice of Mikey 's baby sister , Julie , and Richard Pryor will be the voice of his pal , Eddie . John Travolta and Kirstie Alley are back as the proud parental figures in the film , due this Christmas from Tri-Star . BETH KLEID
