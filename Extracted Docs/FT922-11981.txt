TRIBUNE CO , the US newspaper and television group , suffered a decline in 1992 first-quarter profits but registered a welcome increase in advertising revenues , Reuters reports . It was Tribune 's first year-on-year increase in quarterly advertising revenues since the 1990 first quarter . Tribune 's first-quarter earnings fell to Dollars 11.3m , or 17 cents a share , from Dollars 16.9m , or 26 cents , a year ago . Revenues of Dollars 471.4m were slightly higher than revenues in the 1991 quarter , helped by advertising income which increased by 2 per cent .
