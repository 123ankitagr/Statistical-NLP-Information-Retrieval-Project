Angola 's Unita rebels yesterday said they had shot down a government helicopter and killed seven soldiers in the northern oil-producing enclave of Cabinda , Reuter reports from Sao Tome and Johannesburg . Earlier , the United Nations World Food Programme ( WFP ) said it had suspended all relief flights in Angola after one of its planes was shot down this week . 'We hope to resume the flights as soon as guarantees for the safety of our flights can be provided by all parties concerned , ' a WFP spokeswoman said in Harare .
