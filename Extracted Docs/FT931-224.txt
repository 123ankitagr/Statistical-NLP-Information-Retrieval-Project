american airlines triggered another round of transatlantic price cuts yesterday by trimming another pounds 10 from its economy fares between the uk and the us its london to new york fare is now pounds 249 it is also offering pounds 30 return fares for onward flights to some other us cities daniel green writes the cuts follow reductions announced last week by all the main airlines linking the us and the uk ba and virgin atlantic said they would not change their tariffs in response to the move virgin's fare is already pounds 249, and ba said its pounds 259 fare includes a pounds 10 security charge that is extra on the other airlines virgin conceded that its customers pay pounds 10 more than the published fare
