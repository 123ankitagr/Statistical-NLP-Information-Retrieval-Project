FISHER and Paykel , the New Zealand refrigerator-maker , has announced a 46 per cent first-half profit lift to NZDollars 10.57m ( USDollars 5.87m ) , helped by a substantial boost in sales to Australia , writes Terry Hall in Wellington . Directors said that while New Zealand sales overall fell by 3 per cent they rose 42 per cent in Australia , where the company set up a new plant last year .
