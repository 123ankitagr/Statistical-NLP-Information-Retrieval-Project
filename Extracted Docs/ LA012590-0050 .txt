Qintex Entertainment Inc. : The Beverly Hills firm posted a net loss of $ 33.2 million for the year ended July 31 , 1989 . Revenue totaled $ 86 million . The firm , which filed for bankruptcy protection last Oct. 19 , said prior-year figures are not comparable because the company was formed in 1988 by the merger of Hal Roach Studios Inc. and Robert Halmi Inc .
