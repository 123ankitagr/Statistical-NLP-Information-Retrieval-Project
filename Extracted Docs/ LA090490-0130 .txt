Supreme Court nominee David H. Souter today received the highest rating from the American Bar Assn . 's judicial screening panel , the Justice Department said . Souter received a unanimous `` well-qualified '' rating from the ABA 's Standing Committee on Federal Judiciary , according to a letter from the panel to the Justice Department . Souter , a former member of the New Hampshire Supreme Court , was nominated by President Bush in July for the vacancy created by the retirement of Associate Justice William J. Brennan Jr. Souter received a similar `` well-qualified '' rating earlier this year when he was nominated for a seat on the U.S. 1st Circuit Court of Appeals in Boston .
