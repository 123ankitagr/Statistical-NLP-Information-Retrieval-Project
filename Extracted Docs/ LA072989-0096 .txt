American Express Apologizes to Safra : American Express Co. said it apologized to international banker Edmond Safra for what it called `` an unauthorized and shameful effort '' to encourage false media reports that Safra 's banks had been involved in money laundering . American Express also agreed to pay $ 4 million to charity as part of its unusual apology . In letters released by the two sides , American Express Chairman James D. Robinson III said people `` acting on behalf of American Express began an unauthorized and shameful effort to use the media to malign '' Safra and Republic National Bank of New York , in which Safra holds a 33 % stake .
