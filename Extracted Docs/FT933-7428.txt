mr christopher bailey outgoing chairman of bristol channel ship repairers has sold 4.4 m shares owned by his family interests at 10 p each to the norwegian company which won control of the uk ship management company earlier this year the shares were suspended at 8 p on thursday at the company's request because it had started talks with mr andreas o ugland chairman-designate and other members of his family regarding the proposed acquisition of a fleet of vessels and a ship management business the ugland family interests now have a total of 23.7 m shares some 29.7 per cent of the share capital
