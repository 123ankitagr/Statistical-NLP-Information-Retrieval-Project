COLES Myer , the Australian retailer , yesterday said the NZDollars 200m flotation of its New Zealand supermarket chain Progressive Enterprises had been oversubscribed , Reuter reports from Sydney . Mr Solomon Lew , chairman , said the float of 60 per cent of Progressive was 'highly successful ' and that Coles would issue additional shares to fill all public applications . Based on the offer price of NZDollars 2 , Progressive Enterprises will have a market capitalisation of NZDollars 332m , making it the 14th ranking company on the New Zealand Stock Exchange .
