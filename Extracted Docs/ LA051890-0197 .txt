A documentary of blues man B . B . King 's life , produced for the British Broadcasting Co. 's `` Omnibus '' television series , had its world premiere in his hometown . Proceeds from $ 10 ticket sales for the one-hour film will go to Indianola 's parks . Nearly 100 people turned out for the film , which was shown at the Mid-Delta Arts Assn . theater . The film has yet to be broadcast in England . Much of the film about the blues musician was filmed in Indianola . Many scenes of last year 's concert in Fletcher Park are included , as are local landmarks and citizens . The film reviews King 's childhood in Indianola . King is giving a benefit concert June 1 , with proceeds going to parks in the town of 8,200 residents in west-central Mississippi .
