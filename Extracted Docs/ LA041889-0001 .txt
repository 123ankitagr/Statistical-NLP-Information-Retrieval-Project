Taiwan 's first athletic delegation to China in four decades arrived in Beijing to attend the Asian Junior Gymnastics Championships . The 15 athletes , accompanied by 12 coaches and officials , will compete for three days starting Friday , according to Chan Te-chi of the Chinese Taipei Olympic Committee . The visit marks the first time that Taiwan will be represented at a mainland sports event since 1949 , when the Nationalists retreated from mainland China after defeat by the Communists .
