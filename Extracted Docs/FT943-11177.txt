The Oslo Chamber Music Festival , founded in 1989 by Norwegian violinist Arve Tellefsen , devotes its programme this year to Britain . The opening concert on Friday features songs by Dowland and Vaughan Williams , and later programmes range from Byrd and Bridge to David Matthews and Oliver Knussen . The Nash and Hilliard Ensembles are taking part , while Norwegian cellist Truls Mork plays the Elgar concerto .
