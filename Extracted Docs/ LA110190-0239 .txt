A traffic reporter and pilot were killed early today when their single-engine plane crashed into an embankment along a busy freeway , authorities said . The plane crashed shortly before 7:45 a.m. about a quarter-mile north of Via de la Valle and Interstate 5 , in the Solana Beach area , barely missing a child day care center . The Grumman aircraft was being leased by Metro Traffic Control . The plane , with a pilot and reporter aboard , was circling over an accident under investigation by the California Highway Patrol near Via de la Valle when it went down .
