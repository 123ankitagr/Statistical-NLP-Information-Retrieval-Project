Showtime Networks Inc. : The pay-television company which has been fighting an uphill battle against industry leader Home Box Office , announced that it has secured non-exclusive rights to about 60 films from Columbia Pictures and Tri-Star Pictures . Showtime said it also secured exclusive rights to movies which will be produced by former MCA and Columbia executive Frank Price and distributed by Tri-Star . Showtime is a subsidiary of Viacom Inc. , which recently agreed to sell a 50 % interest in the pay-TV company to Tele-Communications Inc .
