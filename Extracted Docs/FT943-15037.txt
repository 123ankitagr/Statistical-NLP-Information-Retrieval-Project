a campaign to introduce legislation to cut the amount of road traffic was launched yesterday by friends of the earth the green party and plaid cymru the organisations are seeking all-party support for a traffic reduction bill to stabilise road traffic volumes at 1990 levels by 2000 and to reduce them by 10 per cent 2010 they plan to put down an early-day motion later this week to build support both locally and nationally and hope to persuade an mp successful in the private members ballot to put forward a bill later in the year
