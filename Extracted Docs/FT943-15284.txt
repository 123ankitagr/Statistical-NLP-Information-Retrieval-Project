Northern Foods has sold Mayhew Chicken to Grampian Country Food Group for about Pounds 11.5m cash , including working capital at valuation . The disposal of Mayhew , which produces fresh whole birds and portions , together with a wide range of frozen added value products , ends Northern's involvement in the poultry sector . It is in line with the group 's strategy of concentrating on sectors where it has substantial market share and lowest cost producer status .
