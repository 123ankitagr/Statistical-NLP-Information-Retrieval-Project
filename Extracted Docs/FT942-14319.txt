ansaldo volund a danish subsidiary of ansaldo the italian statecontrolled engineering company and chunghsin electric machinery its taiwanese partner have won contracts worth dollars 300m pounds 2054m to build wastetoenergy incinerators for two taiwanese cities andrew hill writes from milan the orders are worth about dollars 200m to chunghsin and dollars 100m to ansaldo volund for a fouryear project which will generate electricity for the equivalent of 20000 families in the cities of pali and hsinchu ansaldo which is controlled by the italian state engineering group finmeccanica said the award would give ansaldo volund 40 per cent of the taiwanese market for incinerating urban waste or 50 per cent if the share of nkk the japanese company to which the danish group has granted a licence is included the group has won orders for two other incinerators in taiwan
