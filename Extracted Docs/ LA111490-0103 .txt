Forward Roy Tarpley will miss the rest of the Dallas Mavericks ' season after ligament damage was discovered in his right knee Tuesday . The damage was found during arthroscopic surgery . Tarpley averaged 20 points and 11 rebounds in five games and was shooting 54 % before he was injured during Friday 's victory overOrlando . An arthroscopy also was performed on the right knee of Maverick guard Fat Lever . He is expected to be sidelined from four to six weeks .
