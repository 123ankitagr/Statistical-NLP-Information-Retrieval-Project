BRITISH companies underrate the standing in which they are held by Japanese business customers , according to a survey from Touche Ross , the accountancy firm , released yesterday . The survey of 100 British and Japanese companies showed that all felt relations had remained stable or improved over the past five years . Japanese companies said UK suppliers had improved their communication skills , quality and attention to customers .
