More than a thousand worshipers cheered as Father George A. Stallings Jr. called for a boycott of businesses that advertise in the Washington Post , and he denounced allegations published in the Post that he had a sexual relationship 12 years ago with an altar boy . `` We 're going to get you , Washington Post , because of your sleazy journalism , because of your obsession to create a story where there is no story , '' the dissident Roman Catholic priest told a packed auditorium . Post Managing Editor Leonard Downie Jr. later said in reply : `` We believe we have treated the Rev . Stallings fairly . ''
