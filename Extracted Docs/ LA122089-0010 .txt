Kurosawa to be Honored : Japanese film maker Akira Kurosawa will receive an honorary award from the Academy of Motion Picture Arts and Sciences at the 62nd Annual Academy Awards show on March 26 . Kurosawa , 79 , will be recognized `` for his cinematic accomplishments that have inspired , delighted , enriched and entertained worldwide audiences and influenced film makers throughout the world . '' Kurosawa 's 1951 production of `` Rashomon '' won the Oscar for Best Foreign Language Film and is generally credited with turning the western world 's attention to the work of Japanese film makers .
