please be advised that falana speaks as an individual with ms and not as a spokesperson for the national multiple sclerosis society ms is a remarkably complex disease that unfortunately still has no cure and only limited treatment in many cases the disease is progressive in others people will suffer severe symptoms and then return to close to their original functioning the factors which contribute to the diseases course are still largely unknown there are an estimated 250000 people in the united states with ms and the methods they use to deal with it are as varied as the people themselves if the disease progresses it certainly isnt a negative reflection upon their will faith or desire to heal we hope that the day will soon arrive when we can finally put this disease to rest carl t white chairman national multiple sclerosis society
