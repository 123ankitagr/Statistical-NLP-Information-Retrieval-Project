The City Council has decided to establish a program to recycle paper and other materials used at City Hall . The council directed City Administrator Louis Sheppard to develop a recycling and conservation program in the next few weeks . In addition to saving office paper for recycling , the city will try to use two-sided printing whenever possible . Sheppard also is considering issuing coffee mugs to employees to reduce the number of disposable cups used at City Hall . `` We need to determine if all the problems are being addressed before we proceed , '' he said .
