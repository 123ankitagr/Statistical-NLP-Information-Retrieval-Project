Universal , Cosby Sign Pact : Universal Pictures has signed a two-year deal with TV sitcom star Bill Cosby , who will produce and star in several movies . The pact gives Universal first look at all feature films developed by Cosby , whose television series , `` The Cosby Show , '' has been at the top , or near the top , of the ratings for five years . The first project under the agreement is `` Loose Shoes . ''
