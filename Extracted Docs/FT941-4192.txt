spain's underlying inflation which excludes the more volatile price movements of fresh foods and energy fell by 0.2 per cent in february to give a year-on year rise of 4.9 per cent the lowest rate since april 1988 higher fresh food prices kept headline inflation unchanged to give a year-on-year rate of 5 per cent the underlying rate was helped by a fall from 6.4 per cent to 5.9 per cent in the traditionally high-inflation services component of the index the extent of this fall indicated more moderate unit labour costs as well as slack domestic demand analysts believe inflation will fall rapidly in the coming months madrid securities firm fg forecasts a headline annual rate of 4.3 per cent by june and 3.8 per cent by the end of the year germany's m3 money supply grew by a revised annual rate of 21.2 per cent in january the bundesbank's target growth range for m3 in 1994 is 4-6 per cent
