Three suspected members of a Communist death squad shot and killed the mayor of a town just outside Manila today , the military reported . Mayor Felino Maquinay of Carmona died on the spot with three bullet wounds in the head , said Capt . Romeo Gullas , provincial operations chief of the Philippine Constabulary . Three unidentified youths , believed to be members of the Communist New People 's Army , approached Maquinay as he was having lunch and opened fire at close range with pistols , Gullas said . Maquinay 's bodyguard and his driver , too surprised by the attack to protect him , were unharmed , Gullas added . The gunmen fled in a commandeered car .
