three orange county residents placed among the winners of the 9 th annual music city song festival a national competition for profesional and amateur singers and songwriters william g hinkle of laguna niguel won a 500 first prize in the middle-of-the road adult contemporary vocal category for his song i'm all brand new ronald valdez of fountain valley came in second in the amateur pop top 40 songwriting category for distant heart co-written with susan stevens of north hollywood doreen campbell of cypress placed third in the pop top 40 song lyrics competition for her lyrics to how did i fall for you the nashville-based competition is judged by panels of music-industry professionals who review taped entries or lyric sheets
