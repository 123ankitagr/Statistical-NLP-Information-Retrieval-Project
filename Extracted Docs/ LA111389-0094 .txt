Bill Craven , Pacifica High School football coach , said he will coach the school 's varsity baseball team next season . `` I agreed to coach the baseball team for one season , '' he said . Craven replaced Art Michalik as Pacifica 's football coach in 1976 and has since won eight league titles . He replaces Kent Bothwell as the school 's baseball coach . Bothwell resigned to become football coach at Palmdale . Craven coached baseball at La Quinta from 1971-73 , winning one league title and twice finishing second . His teams qualified for the Southern Section playoffs all three seasons . TOM HAMILTON
