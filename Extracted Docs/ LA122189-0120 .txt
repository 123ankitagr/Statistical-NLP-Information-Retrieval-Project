Angel outfielder Claudell Washington was arrested Wednesday for investigation of spousal battery , police said . Washington , 35 , was arrested after his wife , Cynthia , called police , Orinda Police Sgt . Hank Davis said . Under California law , Washington was charged with a felony because his wife had visible injuries , Davis said , adding that bail was set at $ 2,500 . Washington 's wife suffered a `` knot on the head , a cut lip and she might have a black eye , '' according to Davis , who said she was taken to John Muir Memorial Hospital for treatment but was not admitted . Washington was arrested at his home and offered no resistance , Davis said . He and his wife have been married since 1975 .
