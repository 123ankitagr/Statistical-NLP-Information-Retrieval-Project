total motor traffic in 1993 was about 0.5 per cent lower than in 1992, say provisional figures issued by the department of transport yesterday car traffic fell almost 0.5 per cent over the same period while light van and goods vehicle traffic was 1 per cent lower traffic on motorways and trunk roads remained unchanged traffic on principal and minor roads fell 1 per cent from 1992 to 1993 total motor traffic in the fourth quarter of 1993 was 4 per cent higher than at the same time in 1992, with goods vehicle traffic up 5 per cent
