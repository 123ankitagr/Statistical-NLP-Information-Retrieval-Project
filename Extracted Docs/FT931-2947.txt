Peugeot , the French carmaker , has overtaken Italian and Japanese competition to strike an agreement in principle for a joint venture with the Indian car group , Premier Automobiles , William Dawkins reports from Paris . Peugeot and Premier Automobiles have signed a memorandum of understanding to set up an equally owned car assembly company in India , to produce Peugeot vehicles in 12 to 18 months from now . The joint venture will assemble around 60,000 cars annually at first . The partners are still finalising the choice of models .
