A Japanese firm , Balzak Corp. of Osaka , has purchased a three-story , 63,888-square-foot building at 225 S. Sepulveda Blvd. , Manhattan Beach , for $ 16 million from Treehouse Associates of Morristown , N.J . The 3-year-old building was developed by Loomis & Eick . Balzak was represented by the Seeley Co. , and Treehouse was represented by Coldwell Banker Commercial Real Estate Services .
