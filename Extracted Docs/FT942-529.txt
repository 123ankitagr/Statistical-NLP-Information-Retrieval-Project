Belgium yesterday released an Italian held on fraud charges connected with the French company Schneider . Mr Valentino Foti , was released without bail on Friday after completion of investigations in Italy , the public prosecutor 's office said . Schneider chairman , Mr Didier Pineau-Valencienne , was released on June 7 on bail after spending 11 days in jail . The two men were arrested in Belgium on charges of fraud and swindling in a case which arose from complaints filed by minority shareholders in two Schneider subsidiaries in Belgium . Mr Schneider and Mr Pineau-Valencienne deny all charges . Mr Foti 's lawyer also has denied the charges against his client .
