Product line sold : Pacific Scientific Co. , an aerospace and industrial products manufacturer in Newport Beach , has sold its line of sensitive relay products to Communications Instruments Inc. in Fairview , N.C. , for $ 2 million . The product line accounted for $ 3 million in sales in 1989 . Richard V. Plat , a Pacific Scientific spokesman , said the company sold the operation because it wants to concentrate on its line of products for the electric utility industry . Communications Instruments plans to build the Sigma relay lines at its plant in North Carolina .
