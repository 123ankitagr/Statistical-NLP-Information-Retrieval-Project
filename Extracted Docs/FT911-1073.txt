there are too many places to have a pint in ireland the irish vintners federation says the proliferation of pubs in the country is causing many landlords serious hardship in county cavan one village has 11 bars chasing the business of only 100 drinkers in ballaghaderreen in county roscommon there are 32 bars a hotel and an off-licence for a population of only 1,400 the vintners say there should be fewer pubs but longer opening hours to cater for those with long thirsts
