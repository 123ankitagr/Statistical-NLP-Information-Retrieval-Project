Arizona State sophomore outfielder Mike Kelly was named recipient of the R.E . `` Bob '' Smith award in Houston as voted by the National Collegiate Baseball Writers Assn . Kelly , a graduate of Los Alamitos High , has been named the National Player of the Year by Baseball America , Collegiate Baseball and The Sporting News . Kelly is only the second player in ASU history to hit 20 home runs and steal 20 bases in the same season . The other is Atlanta Braves outfielder Oddibe McDowell . Kelly hit .376 with 21 homers and 82 RBIs this past season .
