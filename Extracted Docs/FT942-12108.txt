Brand manufacturers have warned that they will reconsider UK investment plans unless curbs are put on supermarkets ' own label 'look-alike ' products . The manufacturers are angry at a government decision not to amend the trademarks bill to restrict the use of 'look-alikes ' . The warning was given by Coca Cola and members of the British Producers and Brand Owners Group . Ministers say it is too late to amend the bill , which was recently given a second reading in the Commons . Report , Page 9
