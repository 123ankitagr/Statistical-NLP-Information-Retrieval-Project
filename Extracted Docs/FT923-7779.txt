Sun Alliance is again offering its Capital Protector Bond , a single premium investment bond with a capital back guarantee after the first four years . There are restrictions on income and surrender during the first four years and the cost of providing the protection will hold back growth on the bond if the stock market rises . Minimum investment is Pounds 5,000 . Those who apply before September 15 will get an enhanced ( 101.5 per cent ) allocation of units - an extra 1 per cent is available to those who invest over Pounds 15,000 .
