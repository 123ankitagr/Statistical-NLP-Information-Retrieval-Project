Cold air that blanketed much of the eastern half of the nation brought record low temperatures to Florida . In Tallahassee , the mercury dipped to a record low of 34 degrees . Melbourne had a record low of 49 , and Miami tied a record with 59 . Temperatures dipped below freezing in the Great Lakes and the northern Ohio Valley . Ft. Wayne , Ind. , reported a record low of 27 . Temperatures were in the 40s in northern New England , where the fall foliage season is just past its peak .
