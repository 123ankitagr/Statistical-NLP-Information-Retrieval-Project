HemaCare Corp. said its earnings more than tripled to $ 178,657 in the first quarter that ended March 31 from $ 55,491 a year earlier . The Sherman Oaks company , which sells blood products and provides blood banking , said its revenue rose 19 % to $ 1.3 million . The company 's earnings rose mainly because of a big jump in providing hemapheresis , or blood treatment services , in the Los Angeles area .
