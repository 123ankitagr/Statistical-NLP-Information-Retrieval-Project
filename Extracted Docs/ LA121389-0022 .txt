The space shuttle Columbia will not be ready for a scheduled blastoff Monday on a 10-day flight , but officials at Kennedy Space Center said they are confident the shuttle will make it into orbit before Christmas Eve . For launch Monday , Columbia 's countdown would have to begin Friday . But Larry Ellis , the NASA manager in charge of the shuttle 's launch processing , said work at the launch pad would not be completed in time . The goal of the mission is the launch of a communications satellite and the retrieval of a scientific satellite .
