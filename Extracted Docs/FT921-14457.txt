ERICSSON , Sweden 's leading telecommunications company , announced yesterday it had won a SKr862m ( Pounds 83.8m ) order from Telecel , the Portuguese communication group to instal a national private digital mobile telephone system in Portugal . As sole supplier , Ericsson hopes that the system will come into commercial service next October . The system plans to cover 150,000 subscribers and 85 per cent of the country by 1996 . In a statement Ericsson said that Portugal was the 14th European country to select the company as a supplier of mobile telephone systems . It added that the order also represents ' a strategic breakthrough ' for Ericsson in Portugal for the supply of the company 's AXE switch gear .
