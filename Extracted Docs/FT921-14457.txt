ericsson sweden's leading telecommunications company announced yesterday it had won a skr862m pounds 83.8 m order from telecel the portuguese communication group to instal a national private digital mobile telephone system in portugal as sole supplier ericsson hopes that the system will come into commercial service next october the system plans to cover 150,000 subscribers and 85 per cent of the country by 1996 in a statement ericsson said that portugal was the 14 th european country to select the company as a supplier of mobile telephone systems it added that the order also represents a strategic breakthrough for ericsson in portugal for the supply of the company's axe switch gear
