rossini is the composer dominating the new opera releases this season its a moot point whether we live in an era that has fostered the pinpoint vocal technique and authority necessary to plumb the treasures lying in wait amid rossinis 38 stage works but scholarship at last has turned its furrowed brow to an artist far too long perceived only as an engaging trickster no name appears more frequently on new rossini releases than that of the fondazione rossini the italianbased organization that is gradually publishing critical editions of all the operas one musician who has consistently availed himself of these endeavors is claudio abbado the berlin philharmonics recently appointed principal conductor is both sonically and musically the center of the new recording of litaliana in
