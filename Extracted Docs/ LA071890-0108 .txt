Diasonics Inc. , a Milpitas , Calif. , maker of medical diagnostic equipment has named Roderick Young president and chief operating officer . Young , 46 , replaces Ruediger Naumann-Etienne , who resigned to form an international investment advisory firm . Young was president of Toshiba America MRI Inc. , a former Diasonics unit . Prior to the sale of the division to Toshiba , he was Diasonics executive vice president and MRI division general manager .
