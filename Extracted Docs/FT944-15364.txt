caterpillar the us manufacturer of earthmoving and mining equipment expects its sales in china to increase to dollars 500m a year by 2000 mr donald fites the companys chairman who is in china to sign two joint venture agreements to produce hydraulic excavators and diesel engines said caterpillar sales in china last year were between dollars 50m and dollars 100m caterpillar is investing dollars 30m in the ventures with the xuzhou construction machinery group and shanghai diesel engine the company is also planning to establish a holding company to oversee its china business mr fites said chinas drive to improve its infrastructure including roads and ports provided great opportunities
