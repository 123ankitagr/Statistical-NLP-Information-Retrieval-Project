caterpillar the us manufacturer of earthmoving and mining equipment expects its sales in china to increase to dollars 500 m a year by 2000 mr donald fites the company's chairman who is in china to sign two joint venture agreements to produce hydraulic excavators and diesel engines said caterpillar sales in china last year were between dollars 50 m and dollars 100 m caterpillar is investing dollars 30 m in the ventures with the xuzhou construction machinery group and shanghai diesel engine the company is also planning to establish a holding company to oversee its china business mr fites said china's drive to improve its infrastructure including roads and ports provided great opportunities
