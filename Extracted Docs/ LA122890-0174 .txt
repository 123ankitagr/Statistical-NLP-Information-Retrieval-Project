Donald Trump may be having a cash crisis , but the high-profile developer has nothing but bravura when it comes to talking about his finances . Trump 's Taj Mahal casino in Atlantic City , N.J. , is facing a proposed reorganization since he missed a $ 47.3-million interest payment on $ 675-million worth of high-yield junk bonds used to finance the gaming house . The bondholders had threatened to push Trump into bankruptcy proceedings and seize control of the casino before a compromise was arranged . Yet , none of this seems to trouble Trump . `` Mark my words , '' he said in an interview published in Thursday 's Wall Street Journal . `` In 12 months , '' he said , `` I will be worth more than I was two years ago .
