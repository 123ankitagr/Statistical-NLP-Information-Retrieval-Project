About 540 athletes from central and north Orange County received gold medals after participating Friday in the Special Games at Cal State Fullerton . Two students at Cal State Fullerton began the games in 1986 as a less-competitive version of the Special Olympics , said Mary Ann Faley , one of the games ' volunteer organizers . `` Our emphasis in this is they are all winners , '' Faley said . All participants are awarded gold medals and T-shirts no matter where they finished in the various events . This year , the special athletes competed in a softball and Frisbee toss , several running races , a long jump and an obstacle course . About 300 volunteers from the university and the community helped supervise the event . After the competition , athletes were treated to an afternoon barbecue , with food and supplies donated by local companies .
