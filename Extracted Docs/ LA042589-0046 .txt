Audiotronics Corp. in North Hollywood , which bought Leda Corp. in 1987 from Leda 's founder and president , Joseph Tung , announced that it sold Leda back to Tung for an undisclosed amount in cash and notes . Both companies make audio-visual products , particularly for use in education and training programs . Audiotronics , which said it does not expect to report either a gain or loss on the Leda sale , said the agreement also calls for Leda to be an Audiotronics subcontractor for certain products over the next four years .
