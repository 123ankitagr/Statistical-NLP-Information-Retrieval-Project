Banco Ambrosiano Trial Ordered : Masonic lodge leader Licio Gelli and 34 others were ordered to stand trial in Milan , Italy , in connection with the 1982 collapse of Banco Ambrosiano , Italy 's biggest private banking failure . But Carlo De Benedetti , chairman of the Olivetti computer firm , will not stand trial on extortion charges after magistrates dismissed all charges against him . After a seven-year investigation , the magistrates ordered 34 people to stand trial on charges of complicity in fraudulent bankruptcy and one woman to be tried for aiding and abetting fraud .
