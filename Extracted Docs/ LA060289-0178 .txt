Stanford coach Mike Montgomery has asked that he no longer be considered for the vacant head basketball coaching position at the University of Maryland . `` It 's a great job , and the situation is primed for the right guy to come in and make things happen , but it wo n't be Mike Montgomery , '' said Montgomery , who has coached at Stanford three years . Montgomery , 41 , told the Baltimore Sun that he met with Maryland officials , including Athletic Director Lew Perkins , Tuesday at a `` neutral '' site .
