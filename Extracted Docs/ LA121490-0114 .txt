marcia milchiker has been elected president of the saddleback community college district board of trustees replacing joan j hueter milchiker a laguna hills research biologist was elected this week during a secret ballot at the boards annual organizational meeting she was vice president during the past year it is the first time that milchiker who has been a trustee since 1985 has been president of the eightmember board the president conducts the boards meetings appoints committees and represents the board at various on and offcampus functions milchiker said a focus of her term will be the search for a third campus site in the district in addition to saddleback and irvine valley colleges trustee robert l moore was elected vice president and hueter was elected clerk
