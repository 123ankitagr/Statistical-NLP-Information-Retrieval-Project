SINGAPORE Telecom , the recently partially-privatised post and telecommunications utility , has announced pre-tax profits for the six months to September of SDollars 776.7m ( USDollars 487.4m ) , a 20 per cent rise over the same period last year . writes Kieran Cooke in Kuala Lumpur . ST said turnover in the six months was SDollars 1.53bn , a 14 per cent rise over the equivalent period last year . ST reported an extraordinary profit in the six-month period of SDollars 44.2m from the disposal of shares in Singapore Press Holdings , the island republic 's largest publishing group . About 11 per cent of ST was floated on the Singapore stock market at the beginning of November . Initial share transactions put a market valuation of SDollars 70bn on ST .
