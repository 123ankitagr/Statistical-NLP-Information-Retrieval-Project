Shares in Biotrace International rose 15p to 144p yesterday after the microbiological testing company announced a marketing agreement with Henkel-Ecolab 's P3 division covering the sale of its Uni-Lite systems in France and the Nordic countries . Henkel-Ecolab , a joint venture between Henkel , the chemicals group , and Ecolab , the US hygiene company , is a leading supplier of cleaning and sanitising products in Europe where it has sales of about Dollars 750m ( Pounds 484m ) a year and a sales force of 2,100 .
