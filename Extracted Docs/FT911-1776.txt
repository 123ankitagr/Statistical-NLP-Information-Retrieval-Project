israeli researchers in experiments on mice have isolated a peptide which can prevent the onset of diabetes and which might provide the basis for an early diagnostic test and vaccine against the illness in humans in auto immune diseases the immune systems t cells which are supposed to fight disease attack the bodys own cells when they recognise a particular peptide a segment of protein in the case of diabetes these are the insulinproducing cells of the pancreas researchers at the weizmann institute in rehovot have found that by isolating the appropriate peptide and by giving extra doses of it to the mice the autoimmune attack could be aborted the peptide is synthesised from a protein which is present in human beings weizmann institute israel 08 343512
