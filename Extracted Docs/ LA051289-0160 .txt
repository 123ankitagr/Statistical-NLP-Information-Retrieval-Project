Thomas S. Maddock , chief executive of Boyle Engineering Corp. , has been elected chairman . Succeeding him as president is Henry W. Haeseker , who had been executive vice president and retains the title of chief operating officer . Maddock has been with the Newport Beach-based design firm for 30 years and served as president for 15 years . Haeseker , a 25-year veteran of the company , established its Florida operations in 1974 and was regional vice president for the 4 eastern offices . He was named chief operating officer in 1988 .
