A former Juvenile Hall probation officer will go on trial Sept. 29 on 24 charges involving the alleged molestation of 10 girls who were in custody there . Robert Denny , 48 , of San Diego , pleaded innocent Monday before San Diego Superior Court Judge Herbert Exarhos , who set the trial date . Ten girls , ages 15 to 17 , testified July 31 that they were touched inappropriately at Juvenile Hall while they were incarcerated there for various offenses .
