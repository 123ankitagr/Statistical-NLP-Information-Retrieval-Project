PORTER CHADBURN is to sell British Fly Reels , its Cornish-based maker of diecast fly fishing reels , to Orvis of Vermont , US . Consideration is about Pounds 1.6m cash , subject to adjustment for completion audit , and represents a small premium over the net assets sold . Under the agreement terms , Leeda Group , a Porter Chadburn subsidiary , will continue to distribute fly reels made by BFR in the UK .
