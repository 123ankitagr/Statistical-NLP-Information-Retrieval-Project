Luther Medical Products : The Tustin catheter manufacturer posted a profit of $ 47,187 for its fiscal 1990 third quarter , more than double the $ 20,343 reported for the period last year . Revenue rose 47 % , to $ 854,983 from $ 581,518 . For the nine-month period , the company reported a loss of $ 190,993 ; it lost $ 92,857 for the period the previous year . Revenue for the three-quarter period rose to nearly $ 2 million , 18 % over the $ 1.7 million recorded for the period last year .
