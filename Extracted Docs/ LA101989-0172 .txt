MGM Files Lawsuit Against Qintex : MGM/UA Communications has filed a $ 20-million lawsuit against the domestic arm of its former suitor , claiming Qintex America Ltd. has not made $ 3 million worth of payments for a TV development deal . The complaint filed in U.S. District Court in Los Angeles claims that Qintex has neglected to make payments for August , September and October . MGM/UA claims Qintex agreed in April , 1988 , to a co-financing venture involving American network television programming . The contract is guaranteed by Qintex 's parent , Qintex Australia Ltd .
