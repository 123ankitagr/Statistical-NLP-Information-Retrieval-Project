With the death Saturday of Emperor Hirohito and the ascension of Crown Prince Akihito to the Chrysanthemum Throne , the person next in line to succeed to the Japanese throne is Crown Prince Naruhito , 28 , formerly known as Prince Hiro , eldest son of Akihito . Other heirs to the throne in order are Prince Aya , 23 , second son of Akihito ; Prince Hitachi , 53 , second son of Hirohito , and Prince Mikasa , 73 , youngest brother of Hirohito . Hirohito 's widow , Nagako , 85 , now the empress dowager , is suffering from hip trouble and has not appeared in public for several years . Palace officials made no mention of her on Saturday .
