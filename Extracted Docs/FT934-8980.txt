AMBER DAY Holdings , the discount retailer , has agreed to sell its import and distribution division Dennis Day to its current management . The Amber Day board had decided earlier in the year to dispose of the division and return to its What Everyone Wants core retail chain . Earlier this month the group revealed that pre-tax losses for the year to July 31 were reduced to Pounds 2.09m ( Pounds 7.75m ) . Dennis Day incurred losses , after provisions and before interest and tax , of Pounds 4.6m on turnover Pounds 8.7m . At July 31 , it had net liabilities of Pounds 4.1m . Amber Day will receive Pounds 659,000 of book debts . The consideration for the sale of the shares is Pounds 1 .
