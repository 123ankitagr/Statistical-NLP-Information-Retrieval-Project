To celebrate the silver anniversary of `` The Sound of Music , '' 20th Century Fox will re-release the Oscar-winning musical on Aug. 24 , the studio announced Wednesday . Winner of five Academy Awards , `` The Sound of Music '' made its debut in 1965 and was a commercial hit , grossing $ 57 million over an unprecedented 4 1/2-year run . The soundtrack album , featuring Julie Andrews , sold 10 million copies worldwide . The film will be re-released in Los Angeles and New York only . In addition to Miss Andrews , `` The Sound of Music '' stars Christopher Plummer and Eleanor Parker . The Rodgers-Hammerstein musical follows a singing family in war-torn Austria . The Aug. 23 gala party will be attended by Andrews , the film 's director , Robert Wise , and six of the actors who played children in the film .
