the monrovia aquatics club will remain a citysponsored program at least for another 18 months the city council decided tuesday the community services department had recommended that the 45member club become independent but a large turnout of supporters at city hall tuesday persuaded the council to continue subsidizing the clubs activities for now councilwoman mary wilcox said after 18 months the city will evaluate whether the club should become independent city manager jim starbird said until then the city will help pay for coaches and insurance he said starbird said the city spends $ 2500 or less each year to support the clubs programs
