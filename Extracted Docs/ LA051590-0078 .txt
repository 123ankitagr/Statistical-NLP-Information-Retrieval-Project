A Navy helicopter crashed into the Atlantic Ocean just off the coast Monday , killing all four people aboard , the Navy said . Their bodies were recovered by divers who found the fuselage of the SH-2 Sea Sprite helicopter , a Navy spokesman said . The downed helicopter was one of three en route from the Atlantic Underwater Test and Evaluation Center on Andros Island in the Bahamas to its home base at Willow Grove , Pa. , said Veronica Schauer , a Navy spokeswoman at Jacksonville Naval Air Station . The cause of the crash was under investigation .
