Top-seeded Ivan Lendl , rusty from a post-Wimbledon layoff , was upset by unseeded Malivai Washington in New Haven , Conn. , in the second round of the Volvo International tennis tournament . Washington is ranked No . 103 in the world . He took only 73 minutes to beat second-ranked Lendl , 6-2 , 6-3 . Washington had not previously beaten a player ranked in the top 25 and lost in the first round of his past three tournaments .
