harrods the london department store has appointed mr clive de boer as managing director mr de boer 54 has been acting managing director since april when his predecessor mr peter bolliger made a controversial exit from the store mr de boer joined the store in 1964 as a sales assistant in the table and ovenware department he served at every level of management and was merchandise director before being appointed deputy managing director in 1992 mr de boer said yesterday he was delighted to become managing director when harrods was on the threshold of new and exciting developments in knightsbridge and in harrods international
