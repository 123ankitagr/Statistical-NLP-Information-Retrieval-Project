Country Music Nominees : Rowdy country singer Hank Williams Jr. got six Academy of Country Music Award nominations Tuesday for a duet with his late , legendary father , who got five nods . The duet `` There 's a Tear in My Beer '' was also nominated for top song , record and video . Clint Black also got six nominations , including best album and song for `` Killin ' Time . '' Grammy-winning singer Rodney Crowell joins Williams , Randy Travis , George Strait , Dolly Parton and Alabama in the top entertainer category . The 25th annual awards show takes place April 25 at the Pantages Theater in Hollywood and will be shown on NBC the same night . Beth Kleid
