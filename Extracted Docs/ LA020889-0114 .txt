Odetics Inc. 's net income for the third quarter ended Dec. 31 declined 15 % to $ 131,000 from $ 155,000 for the like period a year ago . Revenue rose 15 % to $ 14 million from $ 12.2 million . For the 9 months , the company posted net earnings of $ 764,000 , a 16 % increase from the $ 656,000 posted for the comparable period last year . Revenue reached a record $ 39.4 million , breaking the old record of $ 37.1 million set during for the like period last year . Odetics , headquartered in Anaheim , is a manufacturer of intelligent machines , including information systems for collecting both digital and analog data , as well as robotic and artificial intelligence systems .
