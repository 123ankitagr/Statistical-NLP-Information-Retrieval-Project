YORKSHIRE Building Society , the 12th largest in the UK with assets of Pounds 4.18bn , is to take over Haywards Heath Building Society . Haywards Heath , which has five branches and assets of Pounds 125m , is expected to incur a loss this year after provisions against loan losses on self-build housing schemes . Last year it made a pre-tax profit of Pounds 104,000 . Yorkshire outbid Skipton Building Society to secure the takeover .
