ITALY yesterday protested to Russia after Mr Mikhail Gorbachev was forced to cancel an Italian trip , Reuter reports . The former Soviet president , barred from travelling abroad after refusing to testify about the ex-Soviet Communist party , said he cancelled the 10-day trip to avoid a confrontation with Russian authorities . Mr Gorbachev was granted a passport on Tuesday but only allowed to use it to travel to Germany for the funeral of former chancellor Willy Brandt .
