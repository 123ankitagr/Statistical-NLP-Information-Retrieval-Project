us and north korean officials meeting in new york agreed yesterday on a date for the two countries to resume negotiations on their nuclear dispute agencies report the date during the first week of august is expected to be announced today after it has been confirmed by top officials in washington and pyongyang the negotiations which began in geneva on july 8 were broken off after only one day because of the death of north korean leader kim ilsung the us is offering diplomatic and economic concessions if north korea abandons any plans to build nuclear weapons
