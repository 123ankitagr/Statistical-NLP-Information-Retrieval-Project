'' Standing in the Shadows of Motown : The Life and Music of Legendary Bassist James Jamerson , '' a biography and song book by Allan Slutsky today was awarded the top prize in the Ralph J. Gleason Music Book Awards . This is the first of the yearly awards , named for the acclaimed jazz critic who co-founded Rolling Stone magazine , that will honor the year 's best book about music or musicians . The awards are sponsored by Rolling Stone , music licensing giant BMI and New York University . Second place went to `` Country : The Music and the Musicians , '' by the Country Music Foundation , edited by Paul Kingsbury and Alan Axelrod . Gunther Schuller 's `` The Swing Era : The Development of Jazz 1930-1945 , '' was third .
