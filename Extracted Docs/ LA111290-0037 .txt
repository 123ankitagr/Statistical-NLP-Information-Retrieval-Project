ON THE JOB : Ken Weir , the Santa Ana test pilot , is still flying , climbing to over 60,000 feet in a U-2 last week . `` I 'm the only guy over 60 who 's ever been over 60 , '' he cracked . . . . Lockheed tried to ground Weir when he turned 60 , but he sued . Although a Chicago court last week upheld a similar age limit for airline pilots , Weir 's lawyers say they 'll have an easier time . Boeing has agreed to raise its test-pilot age limit , `` and we 're negotiating with every other airframe manufacturer , '' said a lawyer . Dave Lesher and Janet Eastman
