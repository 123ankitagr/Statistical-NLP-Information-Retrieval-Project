B of A Parent 's Debt Rating Raised : Fitch Investors Service raised BankAmerica 's senior debt rating to A-plus from A-minus , citing the San Francisco banking company 's turnaround . The New York rating agency credited BankAmerica , parent of Bank of America , with putting more emphasis on West Coast regional banking , which has made it more profitable and lowered its proportion of risky assets . But Fitch added that BankAmerica 's exposure to problem foreign loans remains a risk .
