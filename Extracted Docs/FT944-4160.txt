Travis Perkins , the building materials group , has sold its Kennedys Garden Centres subsidiary for Pounds 10.8m cash in a management buy-out . A new company has been formed to make the acquisition . Mr George Bullivant , managing director of Kennedys , will be managing director of the new company , which will keep its original name . The consideration is subject to a revision of up to Pounds 1m , to be decided on completion .
