President Roh Tae Woo ordered 49 top generals transferred or retired after army hard-liners criticized him for seeking ties with Communist nations and not suppressing dissidents , officials said today . Roh , a former general who played a key role in the military 's seizure of power in 1980 , replaced several hard-liners in key security posts with generals loyal to him , according to Korean press reports . Many of the generals being replaced were allies of former authoritarian President Chun Doo Hwan . Defense Ministry officials declined to say how many of the 49 generals would be retired and how many moved to other posts .
