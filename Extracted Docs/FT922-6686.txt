BAYERISCHE Motoren Werke AG ( BMW ) , the German luxury car maker , is raising its commercial paper ( CP ) programme to DM1.5bn ( Dollars 937m ) from DM500m , Reuter reports from Munich . The increase is being arranged by Deutsche Bank and Dresdner Bank . BMW added that Standard & Poor 's , the US credit rating corporation has assigned an A-1 plus rating to the CP programme .
