REFORM of the legal aid system , including abolition of the Lord Chancellor's Department and the Legal Aid Board , is urged in a report on publicly funded legal services in the 1990s , published today . The report by the Legal Action Group - a legal services pressure group - says the escalating cost of legal aid has led the government into a series of ill-considered cuts . A Strategy for Justice .
