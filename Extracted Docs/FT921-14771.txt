the former british airports authority reported that passenger traffic at its eight airports increased by 3.1 per cent to 5.5 m passengers last month compared with december 1990 british airways also reported continued recovery in traffic last month with a 0.3 per cent rise compared with the same month of 1990 although the latest baa and ba statistics have confirmed a tentative recovery in air traffic after the collapse caused by the gulf war and the recession airline yields remain under severe pressure because of the intense competition in the airline business one of the reasons for the rise in air traffic is the significant discounts on fares being offered by international carriers the discounts have depressed the carriers yields the latest baa figures showed that the traffic on uk
