simsmetal an australian recycling company has agreed to take control of the new zealand operations of pacific metal industries a fletcher challenge fcl subsidiary for nzdollars 20 m usdollars 10.5 m writes terry hall in wellington the companies will form a 50-50 joint venture which will dominate new zealand's recycling of ferrous and non-ferrous materials the fcl unit has been the leading ferrous scrap recycler in new zealand with an annual throughput of 150,000 tonnes the venture to be known as sims pacific metals will be contracted to supply pacific steel the fcl-controlled auckland iron mill with raw and imported materials
