Meyer Schatzberg , 90 , founder of the After Five Inc. evening wear companies . Schatzberg moved to the United States from Austria in 1903 and began working as a dress contractor on 7th Avenue in New York City . At age 24 he established his own company and became an immediate success , selling dresses for $ 1.87 wholesale , much less than his competitors . At about the same time he began mass producing the evening clothes for which he became famous . Today the After Five collection is sold in more than 1,000 stores in 10 countries .
