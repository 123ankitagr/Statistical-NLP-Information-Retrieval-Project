Assemblyman Pat Nolan ( R-Glendale ) told the Town Council on Tuesday that he is working to get an Enterprise Zone designation for Altadena . Under the Enterprise Zone concept , businesses locating within a selected area receive a state tax credit equal to 50 % of the wages they pay employees , who are taken off of government financial assistance . Businesses also receive a sales tax rebate on equipment bought for use within the zone , plus other benefits .
