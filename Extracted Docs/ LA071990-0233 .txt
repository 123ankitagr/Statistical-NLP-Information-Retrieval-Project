Volkswagen AG , West Germany 's largest automobile manufacturer , said today it sold 1.55 million vehicles worldwide in the first six months of 1990 . Sales rose 1.5 % compared with the same period last year , VW Chairman Carl Hahn told the company 's annual shareholders ' meeting . Hahn added he expects pretax earnings for the six-month period to surpass last year 's record of $ 244 million . Volkswagen , the second-largest automobile manufacturer in Europe after Fiat SpA of Italy , produces Volkswagen , Audi and Seat automobiles . Group sales in Europe climbed 1.7 % to 1.18 million vehicles , Hahn said . He noted that since May , Volkswagen has had the largest share of the European market with a 15.2 % share .
