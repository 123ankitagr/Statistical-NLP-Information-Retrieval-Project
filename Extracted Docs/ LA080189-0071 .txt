El Diario Seeks Bankruptcy Protection : El Diario de Los Angeles , a Spanish-language daily newspaper that debuted two years ago , has filed for bankruptcy . The owners are seeking to liquidate under Chapter 7 of the U.S. Bankruptcy Code , claiming assets of nearly $ 32,000 against liabilities of more than $ 23 million . Mexican investors had provided $ 6 million to start up El Diario as Los Angeles ' third Spanish-language daily .
