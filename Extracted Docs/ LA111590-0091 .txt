The Palos Verdes Estates City Council awarded a $ 70,740 contract Tuesday to Gold Construction Co. of Gardena for City Hall improvements . The builders will remodel two City Hall bathrooms to make them accessible to the handicapped . They will also improve the kitchen , create new offices out of an old conference room and convert a vacant office in another building into a new conference room . The acoustics and ventilation systems in the City Council chambers will also be improved . The remodeling is part of an ongoing effort to update city offices . In 1988 , staff offices and the council chambers were remodeled at a cost of $ 116,000 .
