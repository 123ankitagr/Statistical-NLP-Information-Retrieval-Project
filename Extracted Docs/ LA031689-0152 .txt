Brentwood 's honorary mayor , actress Hope Lange , will lead a parade and festival in the community May 28 . `` A Salute to Youth '' is the theme of the annual Brentwood Memorial Day Parade and Festival , featuring veterans and military units , bands and drill teams , fraternal organizations , school youth groups and entertainment and media celebrities . The two-hour parade will travel along San Vicente Boulevard from 26th Street to the festival area near Barrington . Additional information is available through the Brentwood Chamber of Commerce , ( 213 ) 552-0220 .
