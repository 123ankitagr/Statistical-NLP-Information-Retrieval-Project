In `` Keeping Manson Behind Bars '' ( May 14 ) , Martin Kasindorf wrote : `` Outraged middle-class values have reasserted themselves , quite literally with a vengeance . '' This statement reveals a lot more about the attitudes of the author than it does about society . To attribute outrage over sadistic attacks on innocent people to `` middle-class values '' is to side with the fundamental assumptions of the Manson point of view . Tell me , in which social class in America is sadistic murder regarded as acceptable ? LARRY SELK Los Angeles
