Federal Express Moved 'Em Out : The Memphis-based package deliverer Federal Express said it handled more than 2 million items in one day last week as the Christmas season approached . More than 5,500 employees are working in the company 's national hub in Memphis at this time of year . The facility takes up 1.4 million square feet of covered space , or 295 acres . It has 35 miles of conveyor belts to carry thousands of packages through a stainless steel network of platforms , stairs and observation towers .
