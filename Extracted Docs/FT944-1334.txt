South Korea 's Ssangyong Group is to import cement from North Korea , the first direct North-South deal since Seoul lifted a two-year ban on business contacts . Ssangyong said agreement was reached between the group's vice-chairman Lee Chou-bom and North Korean officials during a trip to the communist North to discuss economic co-operation . Meanwhile South Korea has allowed Samsung Aerospace and Korea Bell Helicopter to assemble helicopters for civilian use using foreign technology . Samsung plans to assemble helicopters in a link-up with Eurocopter France .
