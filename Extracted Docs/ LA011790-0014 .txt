Cruise and Rogers Call it Quits : Actor Tom Cruise and actress-wife Mimi Rogers have decided to end their nearly three-year marriage , saying they have been unable to resolve undisclosed issues plaguing their relationship . Cruise , 27 , and Rogers , 33 , were married in New York on May 9 , 1987 . `` While there have been very positive aspects to our marriage , there were some issues which could not be resolved even after working on them for a period of time , '' the couple 's statement said .
