Saddleback College , the top-ranked team in the state , defeated Orange Coast , the third-ranked team , 6-3 , in an Orange Empire Conference men 's tennis match Thursday at OCC . Saddleback ( 5-0 in conference ) won five of six singles matches to clinch the victory before doubles play : Carl Hinds won at No . 1 singles , 7-5 , 3-6 , 6-2 . Ed Peregrino won at No . 2 , 6-3 , 7-5 , Jamil Atacha won at No . 3 , 6-2 , 4-6 , 6-0 , Gavin Fenske won at No . 4 , 6-2 , 6-1 , and Stan Sanders won at No . 6 , 6-3 , 6-2 . Hinds and Saunders won at No . 2 doubles , 4-6 , 7-5 , 6-3 . OCC is 4-1 in conference play .
