El Sol , a colourful two-year-old attempt to topple El Pais from its position as Spain 's biggest daily newspaper , gave up the chase yesterday and closed down , becoming the third Spanish daily to collapse in the last eight months , writes Peter Bruce in Madrid . The closure of El Sol , owned by an ambitious book publisher , Mr German Sanchez , completes a near rout of some six daily general and business newspapers launched during the economic boom of 1987 and 1988 .
