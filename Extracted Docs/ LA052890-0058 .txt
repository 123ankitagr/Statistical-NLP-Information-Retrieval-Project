A summary of Southern California-related business litigation developments during the past week . American Medical Sues Over Sale : American Medical International Inc. filed a breach-of-contract suit Wednesday , seeking $ 900,000 plus interest after the sale of a hospital fell through last year . The case says Jesse J. Umali agreed to buy Glendora Community Hospital from a subsidiary of Beverly Hills-based American Medical last October for $ 8.5 million . Damages plus 12 % interest are sought because Umali failed to make payments on time , the suit says . The hospital eventually was sold to another buyer . ( Filed May 23 , 1990 . Case No . BC001807 )
