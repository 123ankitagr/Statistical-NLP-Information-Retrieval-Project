A jury in its eighth day of deliberations heard doctors and two of three youths on trial describe how a female jogger was nearly beaten to death during a gang rape in Central Park last year . Descriptions of her critical injuries and the gruesome way they were inflicted were contained in testimony read back at the request of the jury trying to reach a verdict on the attempted murder count . Antron McCray , 16 , Yusef Salaam , 16 , and Raymond Santana , 15 , are on trial on 13 criminal counts -- including rape and attempted murder . They are accused of being part of a gang of up to 30 youths that rampaged through the moonlit park during a random crime spree on April 19 , 1989 .
