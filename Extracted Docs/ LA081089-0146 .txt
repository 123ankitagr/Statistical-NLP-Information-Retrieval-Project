Mark C. Wessel has been appointed vice president of international sales for Parker Automotive Corp. 's CarbonClean products . He will be the Costa Mesa company 's liaison with foreign distributors and will be responsible for the development of additional sales in other parts of the world . Previously , Wessel was general manager of Franz Amrhein & Co. , S.A. , the distributor for John Deere farm and industrial equipment in Central America and for Honda Motor Corp. in Costa Rica . Before that , he was general manager and sales manager for Texas Instruments ' Central American affiliate , Corporation Sigma S.A. in San Jose , Costa Rica . Parker 's CarbonClean products are used to clean pollutants from internal combustion engines .
