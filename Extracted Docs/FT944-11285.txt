london united the west london bus company has been bought by its staff in a pounds 22.9 m deal backed by montagu private equity and 3 i the investment capital group it becomes the ninth london bus company to be sold by london regional transport this year and almost completes lrt's privatisation programme which so far has raised more than pounds 211 m london united operates 450 buses in south-west london as well as the heathrow to central london airbus it employs 1,450 staff and had pounds 36.6 m turnover in the year to march 1994
