Big Advertiser Pulls Out : The New York Daily News , its circulation devastated by a month-long strike , has lost one of its biggest advertisers , Macy 's department store , just as the heavy holiday advertising season is getting under way . Macy 's confirmed that it has dropped its advertising in the Daily News . Officials at the striking unions said the A & S department store , another big advertiser , has done the same . The newspaper 's two tabloid rivals , the New York Post and New York Newsday , have reported large advertising and circulation gains since the sometimes-violent strike began Oct. 25 .
