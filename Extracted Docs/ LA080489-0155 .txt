A 20-year-old man on vacation from the Sacramento area disappeared during a predawn swim in the surf along Will Rogers State Beach today , prompting a search by lifeguards and a Coast Guard helicopter , authorities said . The names of the missing man and his friend , also 20 and from the Sacramento area , were not immediately released . The two drove from their motel in Los Angeles to Will Rogers State Beach in Pacific Palisades early today and decided to go swimming about 4:45 a.m. , county Lifeguard Lt. Randy DeGregori said . The man had been drinking heavily , his friend said .
