Rocky Mountain News sports columnist Teri Thompson was released on $ 10,000 bond after her arrest for suspected cocaine possession during a raid on an east Denver house . Narcotics detectives arrested Thompson , 36 , and John Dziki , 51 , after a no-knock search warrant Wednesday night at the house where Dziki and his family live . Detectives found one-eighth to one-quarter of an ounce of suspected cocaine , worth $ 800 to $ 1,000 , in Thompson 's purse , said Sgt . Joe Black of the Denver police narcotics enforcement and public housing unit .
