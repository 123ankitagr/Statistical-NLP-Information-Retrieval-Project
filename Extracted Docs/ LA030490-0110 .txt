Ree Strange Sheck 's article on the Costa Rica rain forest ( `` Alone With Nature in a Tropic Rain Forest , '' Feb. 11 ) has me bemused and confused . In discussing plant mimicry , the writer described a passion flower that had leaves with Velcro-like hairs `` to catch marauding butterfly larvae . '' Pray , how does a larva maraud ? I thought they tended to be on the sedentary side . SARA MERIC Santa Monica
