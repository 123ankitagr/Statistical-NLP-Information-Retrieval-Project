A November completion is scheduled for the new , 45,000-square-foot corporate headquarters of Armor All Products Corp. in Aliso Viejo . The Irvine-based firm paid $ 2,435,000 for the 4.6-acre site in Pacific Park , Aliso Viejo 's 900-acre business park , according to a spokesman for the Seeley Co. , which represented Armor All . Coldwell Banker represented the seller , Mission Viejo Co. 's Business Properties Division . Koll Co. is the general contractor of the new building . The architect is the DeRevere Partnership , Newport Beach .
