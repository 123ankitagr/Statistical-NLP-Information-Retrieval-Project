the united nations security council yesterday demanded that iraq immediately let un inspectors search baghdad's agriculture ministry for weapons information it said baghdad's attitude was a material and unacceptable breach by iraq of a gulf war ceasefire resolution but did not say what it would do if iraq did not comply the confrontation began on sunday when a weapons inspection team was unable to enter the ministry iraqi authorities said information kept there was beyond the scope of un weapons inspections and not covered in security council resolutions
