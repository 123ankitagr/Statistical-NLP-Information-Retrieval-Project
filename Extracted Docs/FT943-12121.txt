The High Court action brought by 3,095 Lloyd 's Names against Gooda Walker syndicates ended yesterday . Mr Justice Phillips reserved judgment . He is not expected to announce his decision before September . The Names suffered losses between 1988-90 as a result of a sequence of disasters including the explosion of the Piper Alpha oil platform in 1988 , Hurricane Hugo in the US , the Exxon Valdez oil spill and the European storms of early 1990 . The Names are suing for the return of Pounds 629m .
