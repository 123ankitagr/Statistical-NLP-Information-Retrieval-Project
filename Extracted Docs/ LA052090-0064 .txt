Doctors suspect Stormie Jones , the world 's first recipient of a heart-liver transplant , is rejecting a second transplanted liver she received Feb. 20 , a family friend said . Stormie , 12 , was scheduled to leave her home near Ft. Worth today for Children 's Hospital in Pittsburgh after her liver enzyme count shot to dangerously high levels . Stormie 's condition has been constantly monitored since she became the world 's first heart-liver transplant patient on Feb. 14 , 1984 . The first liver was damaged by a form of hepatitis .
