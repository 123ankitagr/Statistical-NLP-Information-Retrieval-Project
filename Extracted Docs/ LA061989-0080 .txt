Growers Consider Giving Apples Away : As sales of Washington apples hit their lowest point of an Alar-plagued year , some growers are considering giving away fruit to the poor as a way to reduce supplies and raise prices . One proposal includes taking a truckload of Washington apples to New York City and giving them away to show how desperate the situation is , said Jim Clements , president of the Washington Assn . of Apple Growers .
