al wesson former public relations director at hollywood park and sports information director at usc died saturday at his home in lake san marcos he was 87 wesson was uscs first sports information director a job he held from 1928 until 1942 while a student at usc and a member of the band he wrote the schools fight song fight on which is recognized as one of the traditional marchingband songs in the country wesson served as a navy airman during world war ii and after his discharge he soon became associated with hollywood park he is survived by his wife frances no funeral services are planned mal florence
