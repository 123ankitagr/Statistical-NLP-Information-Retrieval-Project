Four UC Irvine tennis players advanced on Wednesday to the main draw of the Rolex-ITCA Southern California Regional championships at Irvine . Courtney Weichsel , Maggie Powers , Michelle Foster and Biljana Korac each won two qualifying matches to move into the 64-player main draw , which starts today at UCI . Ali Yoshimoto , who automatically qualified for the main draw , will play Barbara Thompson of Arizona State at 9:30 a.m. today . Two Anteater doubles teams , Yoshimoto-Foster and Kelly Goldsborough-Jennifer Mazgedian , also won qualifying matches .
