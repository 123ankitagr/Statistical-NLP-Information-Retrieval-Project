A revolution is about to erupt in that normally placid market , dictionary publishing . Chambers is publishing an Encyclopedic ( sic ) English Dictionary combining both lexical and encyclopaedic information and definitions . So 'bonzer ' ( Australian slang meaning good ) will nestle close to 'Bosnia-Herzegovina ' ( Serbo-Croat for oh what an awful mess ) . It must surely rank as one of the fastest-to-go-out-of-date publications in history . How long will 'scuzzy ' ( US slang for filthy ) or 'humungous' ( colloquial for enormous ) last ? But check out the entry for Macedonia . All it says on 'economy ' is 'market gardening ' .
